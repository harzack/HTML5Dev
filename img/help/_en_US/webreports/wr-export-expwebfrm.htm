Example 1-25: Example of a URL invocation when exporting to a WebForm:

?func=ll&objId=1234&objAction=RunReport&export_location=form&form_ID=5678&appendForm=overwrite&nexturl=...

In this example, WebReport 1234 deletes the contents of WebForm 5678 and populates it with data specified in the SETFORM field of the WebReport.

For more information, see Exporting to a WebForm.
