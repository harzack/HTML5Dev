To drag and drop a constant to the WebReport reportview:

  1. You can drag and drop constants by selecting the appropriate constant from the list, and then dragging the icon to the point in the reportview where you want to insert the constant.

  2. Optional You can open the Constants tab in a new window by selecting the Constant hyperlink to the left of the list.

  3. Optional You can update the list of constants in the list by clicking . This has the effect of updating the list without reloading the page.

     

    Tip:
    Constants that appear in the list with an asterisk, *, next to them are Global Constants. These can be used in the same way as regular constants, the difference being that they are defined in a different WebReport.
