To create a Warehouse Folder:

  1. Click Transport Warehouse on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Transport Warehouse page, click the Add Item button, and then choose Warehouse Folder.

  3. On the Add page, type a name for the Warehouse Folder in the Name field, and then click Add.

 

Note:
If you want other users to have access to the Warehouse Folders you create, you must assign permissions to them individually. For more information, see Managing Permissions.
