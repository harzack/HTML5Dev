To add Workbench items to a Box:

  1. Click Transport Warehouse on the Enterprise menu.

  2. Click the link for the Workbench that contains the item you want to add to a Transport Box, and then click the item’s Add to Transport Box link.

  3. On the Add page, type a name for the Transport Box where the item will be added in the Name field, and then click Add.

 

Note:
You can also click the item’s Functions menu, and then click Add to Transport Box.

To add all items in the Workbench to a box, click the Add all to Transport Box button.
