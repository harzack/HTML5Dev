Transporting Items

Folders, Documents and Compound Documents, URLs, Custom Views, Projects, Facets and Facet Folders can be transported to a target system. Other items can be transported, but require additional checking to determine if dependencies for these items are met. For example:

  • Categories – items that are transported that have Categories applied to them will also be applied to other items during transport.

  • Permissions – Permissions on items are displayed as information only.

     

    Note:
    If the same item, with the same GUID exists on the target system, the permissions will be applied.
