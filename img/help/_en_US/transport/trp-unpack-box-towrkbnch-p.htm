To unpack a box to a Workbench:

  1. Click Transport Warehouse on the Enterprise menu.

  2. In the Transport Warehouse, navigate to the Transport Box you want to open, click its Functions menu, and then click Open.

  3. On the Transport Box Overview page, click the Unpack to Workbench button.

  4. In the Unpack to section of the Unpack page, click the Browse Content Server button, navigate to the Workbench you want the contents of the box unpacked, and then click its Select link.

  5. In the Duplicate Handling section, click one of the following radio buttons:

    • Replace, which replaces items in the Workbench with the item from the Transport Box if they have the same unique ID.

    • Skip, which ignores items on the Workbench that have the same unique ID. These items will not be unpacked to the Workbench.

  6. Click Unpack.
