To deploy a Workbench:

  1. Click Transport Warehouse on the Enterprise menu.

  2. In the Transport Warehouse, click the link for the Workbench you want to deploy, and then click the Deploy Workbench button.

  3. On the Deploy page, verify that the Workbench information and the items to be deployed are accurate, and then click Deploy.

     

    Note:
    To modify an item before deploying the Workbench, click the itemâ€™s Edit button in the Actions column.

     

  4. In the confirmation dialog, click OK.

  5. When the deployment is complete, confirm the status for each item, and then click OK.
