To add an appointment:

  1. In the Navigation Menu sidebar, click the Calendar link.

  2. In the Quick Event Add area, type the title of your event in the Event Title box.

  3. In the Start Time area, click the Date selector icon and then click the date and time of your event in the calendar.

  4. In the End Time area, click the Date selector icon and then click the date and time of your event in the calendar.

  5. Click Add.
