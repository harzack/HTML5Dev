To set your personal photo:

  1. In the Navigation Menu sidebar, click the Member Info link.

  2. On the Community Participants page, click your name in the list of members.

  3. On the Community Member Info page, click the update photo link.

  4. On the Update Personal Photo page, do one of the following:

    • To upload a file from your computer, click Browse, navigate to the image file, and then click Open.

    • To upload an image from Content Server, click Browse Content Server, navigate to the image file, and then click its Select link.

     

    Note:
    By default, photos must be smaller than 50,000 bytes in size. The ideal dimensions for the image are 80 by 80 pixels.

     

  5. Click Update.
