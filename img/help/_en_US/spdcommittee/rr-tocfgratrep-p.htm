To configure rated items reports:

  1. Select Settings on the Tools menu.

  2. On the My General Settings page, click the E-mail Reports tab.

  3. On the E-mail Reports tab, click one of the following options in the Frequency of E-mail Reports list:

    • Immediately

    • Weekly

    • Daily

  4. Click one of the following options in the E-mail Message Format list:

    • Plain Text Body Only

    • HTML Body Only

    • Plain Body with HTML Attachment

  5. Select the Enable check box in the Rated Items Reports area.

  6. Optional Select the Show Reportable Items link to see the item types included in the rated items reports.

  7. Click Update.
