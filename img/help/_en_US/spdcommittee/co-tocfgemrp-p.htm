To configure email reports:

  1. On the Tools menu, click Settings.

  2. On the My General Settings page, click the E-mail Reports tab.

  3. On the My Email Reports Settings page, do any of the following:

    • In the Frequency of E-mail Reports list, click the frequency at which you want to receive email reports.

    • In the E-mail Message Format list, click the format you want for email reports.

    • In the Rated Items Reports area, select the Enable check box to include rated item reports in an email report.

  4. Click Update.
