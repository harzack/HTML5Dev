To email several community members:

  1. In the Navigation Menu sidebar, click the Member Info link.

  2. On the Community Participants page, point to the Send a Message list, and then select Send an Email.

  3. On the Create Email Message page, select or clear the check boxes that correspond to the users that you want to email.

  4. Optional Type the comma-separated email address of each additional recipient in the External E-mail Addresses box, for example, tom@opentext.com, jane@opentext.com.

  5. In the Subject box, type the subject of the email.

  6. In the Message box, type the body of the email.

  7. Optional In the Attachments area, click the corresponding Browse button, select a file, and then click Open to include up to three attachments with the email.

  8. Click Send.
