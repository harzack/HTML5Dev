To configure the community digest:

  1. In the Navigation Menu sidebar, point to Facilitator Controls, and then click Setup.

  2. On the Configure Community Settings page, click the Digest tab.

  3. On the Digest tab, select a date rage in the Date range to display list.

  4. To enable a category, select any one of the following Enabled check boxes:

    • Logins

    • New Members

    • Deleted Members

    • Most Viewed Items

  5. To specify which details to show, select any of the following check boxes for each category, except Most Viewed Items:

    • Date

    • Name

    • Role

    • E-mail

    • Phone

  6. If you enabled the Most Viewed Items category, click the number of items to display in the community digest in the Number of items to display list.

  7. Click Finished.
