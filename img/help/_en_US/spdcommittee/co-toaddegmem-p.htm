To add or remove expert group members:

  1. In the Navigation Menu sidebar, click the Member Info link.

  2. On the Community Participants page, click the Expert Area link in the Related Links sidebar.

  3. On the Community Experts page, click the add/edit members link for an expert group.

  4. On the Community Add Members page, do either of the following:

    • To add a community user to an export group, navigate to find the user you want to add, select the appropriate Expert check box, and then click Submit.

    • To remove a user from an export group, click the <user_name> link in the Current Experts area, and then click Remove.

  5. Click Done.
