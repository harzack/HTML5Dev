To accept expert group membership requests:

  1. In the Navigation Menu sidebar, click the Member Info link.

  2. On the Community Participants page, click the There are membership requests pending link and select the check boxes for the users you want to accept.

  3. Click Accept.

  4. On the Create Email Message page, do one of the following:

    • Type a message welcoming the new members to the expert group in the Message box, and then click Send.

    • Click Continue Without Sending.
