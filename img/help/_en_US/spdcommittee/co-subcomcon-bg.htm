Subscribing to a community means that you “sign up” to be notified by email of any changes made to the community or its content. Subscriptions are a convenient way to stay in touch and up-to-date with your community. You can cancel community or community content subscriptions at any time.

 

Note:
Subscription functions are carried out by the eLink module, which must be installed and enabled. For information about eLink, see Understanding eLink. Contact your administrator if eLink is not enabled.

 

Communities contain a number of different items to which you can subscribe such as blogs, questions, and forums. You can also subscribe to a community itself, which means that you will receive notifications about all question activity for the community.

In the case of questions, you will receive notification of all question activity for questions listed in that community topic. For more information about questions, see Using Questions and Answers. For other item types, you will only be notified of new items. For more information about community topics, see Browsing Community Pages and Adding Content.

The following table lists the content that you can subscribe to for each item type.

Item Type Subscribable Content
Community Email, Questions & Answers
Blog Entries
Forum Topics & Replies, Questions & Answers
FAQ Entries

You can also subscribe to a community topic, which means that you will receive an email notification whenever there is an update to that community topic. When you subscribe, you specify which item types you want to be notified about, for example, forums and questions.

 

Note:
Your facilitator might choose to automatically subscribe all community members to any of these items by role.

 

For a community, you can also subscribe to the emails sent to the community's email address. Community email is also stored in the mail archive. For more information about the community email address and the mail archive, see Communicating with Community Members.
