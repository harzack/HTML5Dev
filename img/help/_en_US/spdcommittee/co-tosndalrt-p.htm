To send an alert:

  1. In the Navigation Menu sidebar, click the Member Info link.

  2. On the Community Participant page, click the send alert link for the user to whom you want to send a message.

  3. On the Send Alert page, type a message in the Message box.

  4. Click Send.

 

Tip:
You can send an alert to all members of the community by clicking Send a Message, and then choosing Send an Alert.
