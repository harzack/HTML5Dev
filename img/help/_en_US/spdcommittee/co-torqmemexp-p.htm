To request membership to an expert group:

  1. In the Navigation Menu sidebar, click the Member Info link.

  2. In the Related Links navigation menu, click the Expert Area link.

  3. On the Expert Groups page, click the name of the expert group that you want to join.

  4. Click the Add me to this expert group link.

  5. On the Expert Group Membership Request page, type the reason you want to join the expert group in the Reason box.

  6. Click Send.
