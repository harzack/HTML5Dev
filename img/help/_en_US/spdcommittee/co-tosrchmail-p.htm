To search the mail archive:

  1. In the Navigation Menu sidebar, click the Mail Archive link, if available.

  2. On the Mail Archive page, do one of the following:

    • In the Search for box, type your search terms to search by subject, sender login name, sender last name, or sender first name.

      You can narrow your search parameters by using the By list and Search by date boxes.

    • In the Search content for box, type your search terms to search the content of emails.

      You can retarget your search by clicking a different target in the From Here list and Search by date boxes.

  3. Click the Go button that corresponds to your search type.

 

Tip:
If you leave the from box blank when you search by date, Content Server searches from the earliest items, based on their creation date. Similarly, if you leave the to box blank, Content Server searches up to and includes the most recent items.
