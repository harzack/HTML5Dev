Facilitating a community means to perform tasks that create, configure, and maintain the community. When you create a community, you become its facilitator.

You can configure some of a community's basic settings, such as its name, when you create it. After the community is created, you can configure more settings such as administering the membership, subscribing roles to the community, promoting important blogs, and administering expert groups.
