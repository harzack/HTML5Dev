Configuring your rated items reports includes the following tasks:

  • Setting how frequently the reports are to be delivered.

  • Setting the email message format for the reports.

Report Frequency and Message Format

The report frequency and message format settings for rated items reports also apply to question activity reports and community activity reports. For more information about these email reports, see Using the Community Directory.

Item Types

The item types that are available for inclusion in rated items reports are configured by your administrator.
