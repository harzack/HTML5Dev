To export membership to a CSV file:

  1. In the Navigation Menu sidebar, click the Member Info link.

  2. On the Community Participants page, point to the Tools list, and then select Export to CSV.

  3. On the Export Members to CSV File page, select or clear the check boxes that correspond to the users that you want to export to a CSV file.

  4. Click Export.

  5. Save the CSV file to your computer.

  6. Click Finished.
