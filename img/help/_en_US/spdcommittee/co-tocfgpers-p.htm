To configure your personal frontpage:

  1. On your Personal Frontpage, point to the Personal Frontpage link in the Navigation Menu sidebar, and then click Setup.

  2. On the Setup page, do the following:

    • Click a module name in the Content Modules sidebar and then click Add.

    • Click the Edit button to edit the features in each Content Module.

    • Click the Delete button to remove a Content Module from your Personal Frontpage.

    • Click and drag the Content Modules between the top, bottom, left, and right regions of the page to reposition the Content Modules on the Personal Frontpage.

  3. Click Finished.
