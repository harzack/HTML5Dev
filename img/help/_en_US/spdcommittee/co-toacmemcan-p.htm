To accept membership cancellations:

  1. In the Navigation Menu sidebar, click the Member Info link.

  2. On the Community Participants page, select the check boxes for the users whose membership cancellations you want to accept, and then click the accept link.

  3. On the Create Email Message page, do one of the following:

    • Type a message notifying the users that their membership cancellations were accepted in the Message box, and then click Send.

    • Click Continue Without Sending.
