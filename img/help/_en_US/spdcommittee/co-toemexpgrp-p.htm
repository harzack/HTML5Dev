To email an expert group:

  1. In the Navigation Menu sidebar, click the Member Info link.

  2. On the Community Participants page, click the Expert Area link on the Related Links menu.

  3. On the Community Experts page, click the name of the expert group to which you want to send an email.

  4. On the Expert Groups page, click the E-Mail Experts icon .

  5. Optional Type the comma-separated email address of additional recipients in the External E-mail Addresses box, for example, tom@opentext.com, jane@opentext.com, to add external email addresses to the list.

  6. On the E-Mail Experts page, type the subject of your email in the Subject box and the body of your email in the Message box.

  7. Optional To include up to three attachments with the email, click the corresponding Browse button in the Attachments area, select a file, and then click Open.

  8. Click Send.
