Configuring subscriptions allows you to subscribe roles to the emails sent to the community email address, provided the community is eLink enabled. These emails are also stored in the mail archive. For information about eLink, see Understanding eLink.

When you configure subscriptions, you can also choose whether to subscribe all members of the enabled roles or only the direct members. Direct members of a community are those who are granted membership individually; indirect members are those who are granted membership because they belong to a group that has been added as a community member.
