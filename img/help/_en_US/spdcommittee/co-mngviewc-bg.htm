In addition to adding communities to your favorites, which allows you to manage a list of your favorite communities along with other items, you can also view your Personal Frontpage. This is a customizable page that features personalized reports of community-related activity, such as lists of new communities and lists of the communities of which you are a member. For more information about favorites, see Managing Favorites.

Configuring your Personal Frontpage consists of the following tasks.

  • Choosing which content modules appear on your Personal Frontpage.

    Content modules are components that display small reports on the Community Homepage, such as My Communities or recently visited items, or small pieces of information or functionality, such as favorites.

  • Choosing which links are active in the Navigation Menu.

  • Setting the banner.

You can also choose which columns to place modules in, the order in which modules display, and the layout of the columns, that is, their relative size.
