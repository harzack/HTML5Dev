To email a specific role:

  1. In the Navigation Menu sidebar, click the Member Info link.

  2. On the Community Participants page, point to the Send a Message list, and then select Send an Email.

  3. On the Create Email Message page, select the check box for each role to which you want emails sent, and then select one of the following check boxes for each role:

    • Direct

    • In-Direct

     

    Note:
    The display of indirect members can be turned off by your administrator.

     

  4. Optional Type the comma-separated email address of additional recipients in the External Email Addresses box, for example, tom@opentext.com, jane@opentext.com.

  5. In the Subject box, type the subject of the email.

  6. In the Message box, type the body of the email.

  7. Optional To check the spelling of the message body of your email, click the Check Spelling button .

  8. To include up to three attachments with the email, click the corresponding Browse button in the Attachments area, select a file, and then click Open.

  9. Click Send.
