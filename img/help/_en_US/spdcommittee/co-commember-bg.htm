The Community Participant page contains information about the community members, facilitators, and their contributions to the community. The Community Participant page allows you to perform various functions, such as communicating with members through email and alerts, exporting membership information to a CSV (comma-separated values) file, searching for members, editing membership, and adding and removing members.

There are several ways to communicate with community members. For example, you can do the following:

  • Send an alert, which displays your message to the recipients the next time they visit the community.

  • Send email to one or more community members, to specific members or roles, or to an expert group. For information about expert groups, see Administering Expert Groups.

  • Send email to the community's email address, which automatically forwards the email to all members who have the option to receive community emails, which is set by the facilitator.

When you email community members using any of these methods, the community automatically stores a copy of your message in the community's mail archive, which community members can browse and search.

You can also export community membership information to a CSV file which includes community membership details such as contact information for every member in the community.

Depending how the facilitator has configured the community, you can request a membership, request cancellation of your membership, or request membership to expert groups. The facilitator of the community then decides whether to accept or reject your requests, and can notify you through email. For more information about expert groups, see Administering Expert Groups.

You can search for a member in the community by their first name, last name, log-in ID, or email address. The list can be filtered by role type. Users can also be searched based on their membership in an expert group.
