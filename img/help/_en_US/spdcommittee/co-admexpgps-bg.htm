Expert groups allow you to identify and organize subject matter expertise in a community, for example, experts in product design. After you create and name an expert group, you can add community members. Each user's membership in the expert group is then specified along with the rest of their membership information.

You can also configure your community so that users can request to join or cancel their membership to an expert group where the facilitator accepts or rejects the request.
