To accept expert group membership cancellations:

  1. In the Navigation Menu sidebar, click the Member Info link.

  2. On the Community Participants page, click the There are membership requests pending link and select the check boxes for the users whose cancellations you want to accept.

  3. Click Accept.

  4. On the Create Email Message page, do one of the following:

    • Type a message notifying the users that their cancellations were accepted in the Message box, and then click Send.

    • Click Continue Without Sending.
