To invite members:

  1. In the Navigation Menu sidebar, point to Facilitator Controls, and then click Invite Member.

  2. On the Community Add Members page, add the users or groups that you want to grant membership to the community, and then click Submit.

  3. When all users and groups are added, click Done.

  4. Do one of the following:

    • To send a welcome email message to the members of the community, edit the subject and message, and then click Send.

    • To continue without sending a welcome email message, click Continue Without Sending.
