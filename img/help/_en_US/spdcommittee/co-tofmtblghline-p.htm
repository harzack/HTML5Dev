To format a blog title in a headline:

  1. In the Navigation Menu sidebar, point to Facilitator Controls, and then click Blog Controls.

  2. On the Blog Controls page, click the Headlines tab.

  3. On the Headlines tab, do the following in both the Main Headline and All Other Headlines areas:

    • Click a font size from the Font Size list.

    • Type the number of characters in the Length of Topic box.

  4. Click Done.
