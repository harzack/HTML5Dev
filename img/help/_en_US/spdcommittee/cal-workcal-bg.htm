The community calendar is a utility that provides you with an at-a-glance view of community events, such as town hall meetings and social gatherings. Upcoming events may be displayed on the Community Homepage, or they may only be viewable in the calendar itself, depending on how the facilitator has configured the community.

If the facilitator has given you the proper permissions, you can add, edit, and delete appointments from the calendar. All appointments can be downloaded to Microsoft Outlook for easier personal scheduling.
