To cancel membership to an expert group:

  1. In the Navigation Menu sidebar, click the Member Info link.

  2. In the Related Links navigation menu, click the Expert Area link.

  3. On the Expert Groups page, click the name of the expert group for which you want to cancel your membership.

  4. Click the Remove me from this expert group link.

  5. On the Expert Group Membership Request page, type the reason you want to cancel your membership in the Reason box.

  6. Click Send.
