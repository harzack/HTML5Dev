To add a text module to the community homepage:

  1. In the Navigation Menu sidebar, point to Facilitator Controls, and then click Setup.

  2. On the Configure Community Settings page, click the Edit Text Modules link.

  3. On the Text Modules page, select Text Module on the Add Item menu.

  4. Do any of the following:

    • Type a title for the text module in the Title box.

    • Select the Show title check box to show the text module's title with its body on the Community Homepage.

    • Type the body text of the text module in the Body box.

  5. Click Add.

  6. On the Text Modules page, in the Navigation Menu sidebar, point to Facilitator Controls, and then click Setup.

  7. On the Configure Community Settings page, double-click the text module in the Content Modules sidebar.

  8. Position the text module among the active content modules.

  9. Click Finished.
