To promote a blog to the community homepage:

  1. In the Navigation Menu sidebar, point to Facilitator Controls, and then click Blog Controls.

  2. On the Blog Controls page, click the Add Blog button .

  3. In the Add window, navigate to the blog that you want to promote, and then click the blog's Select link.

  4. On the Blog Controls page, click one of the following in the Report Style list:

    • Featured Blog

    • Featured Author

  5. Click Done.

  6. In the Navigation Menu sidebar, point to Facilitator Controls, and then click Setup.

  7. On the Configure Community Settings page, double-click the name of the blog in the Content Modules list.

  8. Click Finished.
