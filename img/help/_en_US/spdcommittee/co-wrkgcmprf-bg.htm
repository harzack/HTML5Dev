Community member profiles provide you with information about community members. This information includes the date a member joined the community, some personal text written by the user, and a list of the user's recent contributions.

When you view your profile, you can add or edit personal text, set your personal photo, which is displayed next to your name in forum and blog posts, and cancel your membership to the community. For more information about cancelling membership, see Locating and Joining Communities.
