To add a LiveReport to the community homepage:

  1. In the Navigation Menu sidebar, point to Facilitator Controls, and then click Setup.

  2. On the Configure Community Settings page, click the Select LiveReports button .

  3. On the Select LiveReports page, click the Add LiveReport button .

  4. In the Add page, navigate to the LiveReport that you want to add, select the LiveReport's Select check box, and then click Submit.

     

    Tip:
    You can add multiple LiveReports at a time.

     

  5. On the Select LiveReports page, click Done.

  6. On the Configure Community Settings page, double-click the LiveReport in the Content Modules sidebar.

  7. Position the LiveReport among the active content modules.

  8. Click Finished.

 

Note:
Only LiveReports that output a list of nodes are supported on the Community Homepage.
