To request membership to a community:

  1. Click a community's Functions icon, and then click Request Membership.

  2. On the Request Membership page, click the role that you want in the community in the Preferred Role list.

  3. Optional To enter multiple email addresses, type the comma-separated email addresses of your sponsors in the Sponsor(s) box.

  4. Optional Type your reasons for joining the community in the Optional Message box.

  5. Click Submit.
