To... Do the following...
Subscribe to an eLink-enabled discussion Choose eLink on the Personal menu, and then click the Add Subscription button . In the Add Subscription window, browse to the eLink-enabled discussion, and then click its Select link. On the Subscribe to eLink page, click the Subscribe button.
Unsubscribe from an eLink-enabled discussion Choose eLink on the Personal menu, and then click the Edit/Organize button . Select the Unsubscribe check box for the discussion from which you want to unsubscribe. Click the Submit button.
 

Tip:
You can also unsubscribe by replying to an eLink message and typing Unsubscribe in the message Subject.
