To read an email message:

  1. Choose My Mailbox on the Personal menu.

  2. On the My Mailbox page, click the Name link of the email message that you want to read.

  3. Optional Click the Name link for the Attachment, and then click either the Open or Save button in the File Download dialog box to view or download an email attachment.

 

Note:
If you also use a desktop client to read your email, messages will not display in My Mailbox if your desktop client is set to delete mail from the mail server after downloading.
