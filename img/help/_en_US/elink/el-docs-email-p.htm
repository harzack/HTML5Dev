To email a document:

  1. In Content Server, browse to the document that you want to email.

  2. Click the document's Functions icon, and then choose E-mail.

  3. On the E-mail This Document page, type the email address of each recipient in the To field, separating addresses with a comma or semicolon.

  4. Optional Type the email address of each recipient you want to send a copy of this email message to in the CC: field. Separate addresses with a comma or semicolon.

  5. Type a description for the email in the Subject field.

  6. Click the Attachment or Link radio button to choose the format in which your document will be sent.

  7. Type your message in the Message field.

  8. Click the Send button.

Tip! You can also choose users by clicking the Choose User icon and selecting users from the Add recipients page.
