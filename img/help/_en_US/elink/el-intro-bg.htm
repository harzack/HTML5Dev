eLink enables you to interact with Content Server through email. You can use email to participate in discussions; add items to folders, workspaces and container items; initiate and participate in workflows; and receive notifications without accessing Content Server in a Web browser. Also, when working in Content Server, you can email documents to other users and use the My Mailbox email client to read, send, and manage email messages.
