To manage an email message:

  1. Choose My Mailbox on the Personal menu.

  2. On the My Mailbox page, click the Name link of the email message that you want to manage.

  3. Do one of the following:

    • Click the Reply To Sender button , verify the email address, and then click the Send button.

    • Click the Reply To All button , verify the email addresses, and then click the Send button.

    • Click the Forward button , type or select the email address of each user to whom you want to forward this message, and then click the Send button.

    • Click the Delete Mail Message button , and then click the OK button in the confirmation dialog box.

 

Tip:
You can click the Send a copy of sent messages to My Mailbox check box to receive a copy of a message that you send.
