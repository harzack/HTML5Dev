You can use eLink with discussions to post and read discussion items without accessing Content Server. Once a discussion is eLink enabled, discussion managers can automatically subscribe individual users or groups for eLink participation in the discussion. Individual users who are auto-subscribed to a discussion can unsubscribe and discussion managers can re-subscribe those users if necessary.

When you use eLink to auto-subscribe participants in a project discussion, you should ensure that the project roles (Members, Coordinators and Guests) are defined such that users receive role-specific eLink messages. When a topic or reply is added to an eLink-enabled discussion, anyone who has an eLink subscription receives a copy of the message and any attachments by email.

Users can also subscribe to an eLink-enabled discussion, and then unsubscribe when they no longer want to receive eLink messages.

For information about working with discussions, see Communicating with Discussions.
