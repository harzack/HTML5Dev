To... Do the following...
Enable eLink for an item
1.

Click the item's Functions icon, and then choose E-Mail Enable.

2.

On the Enable eLink page, type an email address for the item in the E-mail Alias box.

3.

If applicable, select how the contents are stored in the Content Stored area.

4.

Click the Enable button.

Disable eLink for an item Click the item's Functions icon, and then choose E-Mail Disable. On the Disable eLink page, click the Disable button.
