To auto-subscribe a Discussion:

  1. Enable eLink for the discussion. Click the discussion's Functions icon, and then choose E-Mail Enable. On the Enable eLink page, type an email address for the container in the E-mail Alias field, select the contents to store in the Content Stored field, and then click the Enable button.

  2. Click the discussion's Functions icon again, and then choose eLink Auto-Subscriptions.

  3. On the Auto-subscribe page, select the check box for each user or group that you want to auto-subscribe to the discussion.

    If users have unsubscribed from the discussion and you want to re-subscribe them, click the check box for each user or group that you want to re-subscribe.

  4. Click the Update button.
