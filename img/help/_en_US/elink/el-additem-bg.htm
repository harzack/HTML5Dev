You can add items to Content Server using eLink and your email program, without having to log in to Content Server. You add items by sending an email attachment to the eLink email address for an eLink-enabled container. You can add documents, images, and other items to a project, folder, compound document, or a custom container in Content Server. If you have Reserve permissions for a document, you can also use eLink to add a version to it.

The administrator can configure the system to send a confirmation message when an item is added to a folder or container through email. For more information about configuring eLink, contact your administrator.
