You can manage eLink settings to specify vacation settings, specify the email settings, and set eLink notifications.

Specifying vacation settings lets you stop email delivery of eLink messages from today until the date that you specify. When you specify vacation settings, you select the date that you want to resume receiving eLink messages.

Specifying email settings lets you set the email content format and specify whether or not to receive copies of topics and replies that you post to an eLink-enabled discussion. When you specify the email content format, you can choose either plain text or HTML format; you should select the format that your email program supports.

Setting up eLink notifications lets you specify the events for which you want to receive an eLink email notification; for example, when you are added to a project or a Task is assigned to you. eLink notifications are sent immediately for event changes, which can sometimes be more beneficial than Content Server Notification.

 

Notes:

  • If you choose to use eLink notifications, you must disable Content Server Notifications to prevent duplicate notification email messages.
