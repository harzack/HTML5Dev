The My Workspace is a location in Content Server over which you have complete control. With the exception of the administrator, you are the only user with access to your personal My Workspace. Other users cannot access it directly. However, you can grant users permissions to access or view individual items in your My Workspace. For more information about granting users access to items stored in your My Workspace, see Understanding Permissions.

 

Note:
If your administrator allows you to customize your My Workspace, you can create a Custom View or an Appearance for it. For more information, see Working with Custom Views and Working with Appearances.
