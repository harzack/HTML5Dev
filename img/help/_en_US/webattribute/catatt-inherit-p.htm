To enable or disable Category inheritance:

  1. Click a container's Functions icon, choose Properties, and then choose Categories.

  2. Click the Edit Inheritance icon.

    The Edit Inheritance icon is only displayed if the container is associated with a Category.

  3. For each Category, select or clear the Disable Inheritance check box.

  4. Click the Submit button.

  5. On the Categories tab of the Properties page, click the Submit button.
