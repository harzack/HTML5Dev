To remove a Category from an item:

  1. Click an item's Functions icon, choose Properties, and then choose Categories.

  2. On the Categories tab of the Properties page, click the name of the Category you want to remove.

  3. Click the Remove button.

  4. Click the OK button in the dialog box.

 

Tip:
You can also remove a Category when copying or moving an item. For more information, see Working with General Items.
