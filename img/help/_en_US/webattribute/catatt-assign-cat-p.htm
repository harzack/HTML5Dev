To apply a Category to an item:

  1. Click the item's Functions icon, choose Properties, and then choose Categories.

  2. Click the Add Categories icon.

  3. On the Select Category page, navigate to a Category, and then click its Select link.

    Optionally, edit any attribute values.

  4. Click the Submit button.

 

Note:
For information about editing attribute values, see Working With Attributes.

 
 

Tip:
You can also apply a Category when you add, copy, or move an item. For more information, see Working with General Items.
