To view group information:

  1. From the Global Menu bar, choose Users & Groups on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Users and Groups page, find the group whose information you want to view. For information on searching for groups, see To Find a User or Group.

  3. Click the group's View link in the Actions column.

 

Tip:
To view your own group information, from the Global Menu bar, choose My Groups on the Personal menu.
