To comment on an FAQ entry:

  1. On the FAQ's main page, click the See Details link for the entry.

  2. On the FAQ Entry page, click the Add A Comment link.

  3. On the Comments page, type the body of the comment in the Comments box.

  4. Click the icon that you want to appear next to the comment in the Comment Icon area.

  5. Specify any other general item settings.

  6. Click Submit.

 

Tips:

  • The Add Comments window stays open after you add comments, allowing you to view other comments for the FAQ. To return the FAQ page, you must close the Add Comments window by clicking the Close button in the top right corner of the window.

  • If you want to edit a comment after you already added it, you can do so by clicking the comment's Functions icon, and then clicking Edit Entry. Make your edits to the comment, and then click Submit.

  • You can view all posted comments by clicking the View Comments link on the FAQ page.
