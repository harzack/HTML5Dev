When you subscribe to an FAQ, you sign up to be notified by email of any changes made to its content, for example, when new entries are added or existing entries are changed.

 

Note:
To be notified of new FAQ changes, modify your notification interests. For more information about notifications, see Using Notifications.

FAQ subscriptions require that the eLink module is installed and enabled. Contact your administrator to enable eLink. For information about eLink, see Understanding eLink.
