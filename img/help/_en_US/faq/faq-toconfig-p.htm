To change the settings of an FAQ:

  1. Click the FAQ's Functions icon, and then click Change Settings.

  2. On the FAQ Settings page, do any of the following:

    • Edit the title or description of the FAQ.

    • Select or clear the Enable Comments check box.

    • Select or clear the Enable E-mail Subscriptions check box.

    • Edit a topic name in the FAQ Topics area.

    • Click the Add Entry topics button for each additional topic you want to add, and then type the topic name in the new, empty text box.

  3. Click Submit.

 

Tip:
To change the settings of an FAQ from its main page, click the Change Settings link in the FAQ Console sidebar.
