FAQs are collections of frequently asked questions and the answers to those questions. These sets of questions and answers (entries) are sometimes organized by topic, which makes it easier to locate entries in long FAQs.

You can subscribe to FAQs, comment on FAQ entries, or create your own FAQs anywhere in Content Server, provided you have the proper permissions. In your FAQs, you can write entries or transfer content to the FAQs from community forums and questions. For more information about community forums, see Using Forums. For more information about questions, see Using Questions and Answers.
