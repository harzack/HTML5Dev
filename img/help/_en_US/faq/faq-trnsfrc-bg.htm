Transferring content to FAQs allows you to capture valuable information in a place where it is easily accessible. By transferring content to FAQs from forums, and the answer desk, you take the information out of more cluttered surroundings – where content is constantly being added, edited, and possibly deleted – and move it to where it can be emphasized as important, collected knowledge.

When you transfer content to an FAQ, it gets added to the FAQ as an entry. As with all FAQ entries, you can edit and configure the new entry to conform to the rest of the FAQ. For more information about adding FAQ entries, see Adding New Content to FAQs.

Also, for each entry created by transferring content, users can backtrack to the source of the information, for example, the forum from which the information was transferred.

You can only transfer content to a FAQ if you have the proper permissions. For more information about permissions, see Managing Permissions.
