You can use the Content Server Advanced Search page to search for email-specific properties, such as the sent date or sender name. The following email search options are available on the Content Server Advanced Search page:

Email

Enables you to search for standard email properties, including subject, sender, recipient, and sent or received dates.

Email (Advanced)

Enables you to search for a custom set of email properties that you select yourself.

 

Note:
Your administrator determines which fields are available. Only email properties that are set to be Queryable and Displayable on the Regions tab of the Enterprise Search Manager administration page are available for selection in the Email (advanced) section of the Advanced Search page. The name of the property corresponds to the Display Name of the index region.
