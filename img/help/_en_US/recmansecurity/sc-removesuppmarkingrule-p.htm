To remove a supplemental marking from an item using a defined rule:

  1. Click an itemâ€™s Functions icon, click Properties, and then choose Security Clearance.

  2. On the Security Clearance tab, click the Defined Rules link.

  3. On the Defined Rules tab, click Remove Supplemental Marking in the Action to Perform list.

  4. Click a supplemental marking in the Supplemental Marking list.

  5. Select the month, day, year, and time that you want the supplemental marking removed from the item in the Date to Perform lists.

  6. Optional Type the reason why you are performing the action on the item in the Reason for Rule field.

  7. Click Update.

  8. If you are removing Supplemental Markings from a container with sub-items, click one of the following radio buttons in the Update Options window, and then click Close:

    • Apply changes to current item only

    • Apply changes to current item and sub-items
