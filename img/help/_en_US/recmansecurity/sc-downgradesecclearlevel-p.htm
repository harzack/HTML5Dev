To downgrade the security clearance level on an object:

  1. Click an itemâ€™s Functions icon, choose Properties, and then choose Security Clearance.

  2. On the Security Clearance tab, click a security clearance level in the Current Security Clearance Level drop-down list. The level must be lower than the security clearance level in the Initial Security Clearance Level drop-down list.

  3. In the Review and Regrading Information section, click a date in the Downgraded On drop-down lists.

  4. Type the name of the person or agency who performed the downgrade in the Downgraded By field.

  5. Click the Update button.
