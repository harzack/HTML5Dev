To remove Supplemental Markings from objects:

  1. In Browse View, select the check box for item(s) for which you are removing the Supplemental Marking, and then click the Remove Supplemental Markings button.

  2. On the Remove Supplemental Markings page, select the Supplemental Markings you want to remove in the Available field, use the left arrow to move it to the Remove field, and then click the Remove button.

  3. Click OK.

 

Tip:
You can also remove Supplemental Markings for one or more item(s) in a Collection or Search Result by selecting the check box for the item(s), and then clicking Remove Supplemental Markings in the Actions drop-down list.

 
 

Note:
