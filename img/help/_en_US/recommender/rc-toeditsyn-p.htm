To edit a synopsis for an item:

  1. In the Enterprise Workspace, click an item's Functions icon, and then choose Rate It.

  2. On the Ratings tab of the item's Properties page, click the Synopsis icon, .

     

    Note:
    The synopsis icon appears only if your administrator has granted users permission to edit synopses and if you have the Modify permission for the item.

     

  3. In the Edit the Synopsis window, modify the item synopsis.

  4. Click Update.

 

Tip:
You can also access the Ratings tab on an item's Properties page by clicking the item's Functions icon, choosing Properties, and then choosing Ratings.
