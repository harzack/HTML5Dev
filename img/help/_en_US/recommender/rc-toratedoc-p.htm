To rate an item:

  1. In the Enterprise Workspace, click an item's Functions icon, and then choose Rate It.

  2. On the Ratings tab of the item's Properties page in the My Review section, click a rating for the item in the Rating list.

     

    Tip:
    To remove your review for an item, submit a rating of <None>.

     

  3. Optional If you want to include a comment or explanation with your rating, type text in the Comment or Explanation field.

  4. Optional To remove your review for an item, submit a rating of <None>.

  5. Click Submit.

 

Tips:

  • You can also access the Ratings tab on an item's Properties page by clicking an item's Functions icon, choosing Properties, and then choosing Ratings.
