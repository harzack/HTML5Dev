To edit Participant information:

  1. Open a Project and then choose Participants on the Project menu.

  2. On the Project Participants page, click Edit Participants, .

  3. On the Project Edit-Participants page, modify the information, and then click Submit.

To... Do the following...
Enable or disable Public Access Click Enable or Disable in the Actions column.
Change a participant's role Click a new role in the Role list.
Remove participants from the Project Select the check box for each participant you want to remove.
