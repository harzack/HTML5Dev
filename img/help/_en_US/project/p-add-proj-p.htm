Note:
After you specify whether you are adding the Project using a blank Template or an existing Template, you can finish the procedure without adding any additional information by clicking Finish.

 

To add a Project:

  1. On the Add Item menu, click Project.

  2. In the General Info section of the Add Project page, do the following:

    • Type a name for the Project in the Name field.

    • In the Create From section, select Blank Project.

    • Specify any other general steps, and then click Next.

  3. In the Content section, do the following:

    • Click a status for the Project in the Status list.

    • Click a month, day, and year in the Start Date and Target Date lists.

    • Perform any other optional steps in the Content section, and then click Next.

  4. In the Participants section, do the following:

    • Click the appropriate radio button in the Participants section.

      Optionally, click Add Participant or Edit Participant to add or edit Project participants.

    • In the Public Access section, click Enabled or Disabled.

    • Click the appropriate radio button to determine which types of Project participants receive notifications via email in the E-mail Notifications section.

       

      Note:
      If Notifications are not enabled, or you do not have an email address specified, you cannot set up email notifications.

       

    • Click Next.

  5. In the Presentation section, do the following:

    • Click a radio button in the # of Featured Sub-Items section.

    • Perform any other optional steps in the Presentation section, and then click Finish.

 

Tip:
You can also add a Project by choosing Projects on the Personal menu, and then click Create Project on the My Projects page.
