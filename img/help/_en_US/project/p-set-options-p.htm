To set Project appearance options:

  1. Click a Project's Functions icon, choose Properties, and then choose Presentation.

  2. On the Presentation tab of the Project Properties page, click Options.

  3. In the Options section, click the radio button for the maximum number of featured sub-items that you want to appear in the # of Featured Sub-Items section.

  4. Click Submit.

To... Do the following...
Add a banner image In the 1st Banner or 2nd Banner section, click Browse Content Server, navigate to an image, and then click Select.
Add alternate text to a banner image Type the text in the ALT Text field.
To associate a URL with the banner image Type the URL in the Target URL field.
Specify a News Channel In the News Player list box, click the Channel where the News you want played resides.
