To add a Project participant or group:

  1. Open a Project and then choose Participants on the Project menu.

  2. On the Project Participant page, click Add Participants, .

  3. On the Project Add-Participants page, find the user or group you want to add, click a role for the user or group in the Role list, and then click Submit. Repeat for each participant or group you want to add.

  4. Click Done.
