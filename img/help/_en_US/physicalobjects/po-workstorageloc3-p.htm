To view physical items assigned to a locator:

  1. Choose Physical Objects Workspace on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Physical Objects Workspace page, click the Storage Management link.

  3. On the Storage Management page, click the Locators link.

  4. On the Locators page, specify search criteria, and then click the Search button.

  5. Click the Functions icon for a locator, and then choose View Contents.

 

Tip:
You can specify additional search criteria in the Locator field by clicking the Add Row button , and then typing search text in the additional fields. To remove a Locator search field, click the Delete Row button .
