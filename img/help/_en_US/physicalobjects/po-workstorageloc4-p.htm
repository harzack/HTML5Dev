To delete a storage locator:

  1. Choose Physical Objects on the Personal menu.

  2. On the Physical Objects page, click the Storage Management link.

  3. On the Storage Management page, click the Locators link.

  4. On the Locators page, specify search criteria, and then click the Search button.

  5. Click the Functions icon for a locator, and then choose Delete.

 

Note:
You cannot delete locators that have assigned physical items. For information about removing physical item assignments or removing Locators from physical items, see Storing Physical Items.
