To create a transfer:

  1. Choose Physical Objects Workspace on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Physical Objects Workspace page, click the Storage Management link.

  3. On the Storage Management page, click the Transfers link.

  4. On the Transfers page, click Transfer on the Add Item menu.

  5. On the Add Transfer page, type an ID in the Transfer ID field.

  6. In the Created section, click a date in the drop-down lists.

  7. Specify any of the remaining settings.

  8. Click the Add Item button.

 

Note:
The date in the Created drop-down list defaults to and cannot be later than the current date.
