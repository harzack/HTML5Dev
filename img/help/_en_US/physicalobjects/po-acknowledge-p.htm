To acknowledge borrowed items through an email notification:

  1. In the acknowledgement email notification, click the link of the physical item.

  2. Log in to Content Server.

  3. On the Acknowledgement of Receipt page, click the Acknowledge button.

 

Note:
If the you forward the email to another person, that person will be able to acknowledge it, but you will still be listed as the borrower of the item.

 

To acknowledge borrowed items from the Borrowed Items page:

  1. Click Borrowed Items on the Personal menu.

  2. On the Borrowed Physical Items page, select the check box beside each item you want to acknowledge, then click Acknowledge in the Choose action to perform drop-down list.
