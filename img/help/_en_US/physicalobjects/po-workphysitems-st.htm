In Physical Objects, you can use physical items to manage physical objects. Physical items are electronic entities in a database that represent either a physical object, like a book or CD ROM, or a physical object container, such as a box or folder. Once you have created a physical item, you can monitor its use as users borrow, group, and move the physical items within the Content Server workspaces. You can view the circulation history of a physical item. If you need to locate a physical item, you can use the advanced search capabilities in Content Server. You can also configure your personal physical objects settings.
