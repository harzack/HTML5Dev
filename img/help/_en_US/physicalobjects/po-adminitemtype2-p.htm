To edit the general properties of physical item types:

  1. Choose Physical Objects Workspace on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Physical Objects Workspace page, click the Physical Item Types link.

  3. On the Physical Item Types page, click the Functions icon for a physical item type, choose Properties, and then choose General.

  4. On the General tab of the Physical Item Type Properties page, modify any of the settings.

  5. Click the Update button.
