To flag multiple borrowed physical items for pick up:

  1. Choose Physical Objects on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Physical Objects page, click the Circulation link.

  3. On the Circulation page, click the Borrowed Physical Items link.

  4. On the Borrowed Physical Items page, click Flag for Pick Up in the Action drop-down list.

  5. Specify any of the remaining settings, and then click the Search button.

  6. In the list of search results, select the check box for each borrowed physical item you want to flag for pick up.

  7. Click the Flag for Pick Up button.
