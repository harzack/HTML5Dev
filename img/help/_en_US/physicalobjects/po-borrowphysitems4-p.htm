To borrow requested physical items:

  1. Choose Physical Objects Workspace on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Physical Objects Workspace page, click the Circulation link.

  3. On the Circulation page, click the Requested Physical Items link.

  4. On the Request Physical Items page, click Borrow in the Action drop-down list. Optionally, select the Pre-Approved Security Overrides check box to automatically approve any security overrides.

  5. Specify any of the remaining settings, and then click the Search button.

  6. Click the Choose User icon in the Obtained By section, and then choose a user. Specify this field if the physical item or physical item copy is borrowed on behalf of the user identified in the Borrowed By section.

  7. Click a date in the drop-down lists in the following sections:

    • Borrow Date, which lets you specify a date for borrowing the physical items

    • Return Date, which lets you specify a date for returning the physical items

  8. Specify any of the remaining settings.

  9. Click the Borrow button.
