To assign physical items to a storage locator:

  1. Choose Physical Objects Workspace on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Physical Objects Workspace page, click the Storage Management link.

  3. On the Storage Management page, click the Functions icon for the Locators link, and then choose Assign Contents.

  4. On the Assign Locator page, click a facility in the Facility drop-down list.

  5. Click an area of the facility in the Area drop-down list.

  6. Type a storage locator in the Locator field.

  7. Type unique IDs for the physical items in the Unique ID List field.

  8. Click the Assign button.

 

Tips:

You can assign a Locator to multiple physical items from a Collection or set of Search results, or from the Browse View of a workspace by selecting the check box for each item, and then clicking the Assign Locator button (or from the More Actions drop-down list).

  • You can search for a storage locator by clicking the Search Locators button, and then clicking the Select link for an storage locator on the Select Locator page.

  • You can also assign physical items by scanning a list of bar codes in the Unique ID List field or by clicking the Browse button, and then browsing to a list of unique IDs.
