To assign a storage locator to physical items in a Collection:

  1. In a Collection, select the check box next to every physical item you want to assign.

  2. Click Assign Locator in the More Actions drop-down list.

  3. On the Assign Locator page, click a facility in the Facility drop-down list.

  4. Click an area of the facility in the Area drop-down list.

  5. Type a storage locator in the Locator field. Optionally, you can specify an ID for the off-site storage facility in the Offsite Storage ID field.

  6. Click the Search Locators button.

  7. In the list of search results, click the Select link for a locator.
