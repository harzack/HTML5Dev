To recalculate storage space:

  1. Choose Physical Objects Workspace on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Physical Objects Workspace page, click the Physical Objects Administration link.

  3. On the Storage Management page, click the Locators link.

  4. On the Locators page, specify search criteria, and then click the Search button.

  5. Click the Functions icon for a locator, and then choose Locator Recalculate.

  6. On the Recalculate Locators page, click a storage facility in the Facility drop-down list.

  7. Click an area in the Area list.

    Optionally, select the Increment total space check box to have the total space in non-managed facilities increased.

  8. Click the Recalculate button.

 

Tip:
You can select multiple areas by pressing CTRL before clicking an area in the Area list.

You can also recalculate storage space by clicking the Functions icon for the Locator link, and then choosing Locator Recalculate.
