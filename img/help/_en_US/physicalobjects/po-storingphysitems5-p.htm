To assign a storage locator to boxes in a transfer:

  1. Choose Physical Objects Workspace on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Physical Objects Workspace page, click the Storage Management link.

  3. On the Storage Management page, click the Transfer link.

  4. On the Transfers page, click the Transfers Received radio button.

    Optionally, type an ID in the Transfer ID field to specify a transfer.

  5. Click the Search button.

  6. In the list of search results, click the Functions icon for a transfer, and then choose Assign Locator.

  7. On the Add Locator page, select the check box next to each box you want assigned to the locator.

  8. Specify any search criteria for the storage locators, and then click the Find Locators button.

  9. In the list of locators, click the Assign link in the Action column for the locator you want to assign.

 

Note:
Only managed facilities are available from the Facility drop-down list.

If the box was already in a locator and you removed the assignment and assigned a new one, the free space of the old locator is updated.
