To assign one or more physical items to a box from the Detail View page:

  1. Click the link for a box.

  2. On the Detail View page, click the Assign to Box button.

  3. Do one of the following:

    • Type the name of a physical item in the Assign Single Item field to assign a single physical item.

    • Type unique IDs in the Unique ID List field to assign multiple physical items.

    • Browse to the location of a file with a list of Unique IDs and upload to the field Read from a File.

    Optionally, select the Update Location of all Sub-Items check box to update the location of any sub-items the physical item or items have.

    Optionally, select the Update Status of all Sub-Items check box to update the status of any sub-items that the physical item or items have.

  4. Click the Update button.

 

Tips:

  • You can also assign a single item by clicking the Browse Content Server button, and then browsing to a physical item in .

  • You can also assign multiple physical items by scanning a list of bar codes in the Unique ID List field or by clicking the Browse button, and then browsing to a list of unique IDs.
