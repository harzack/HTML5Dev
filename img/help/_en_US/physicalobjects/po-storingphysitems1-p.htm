To assign a storage locator to a physical item:

  1. Click the Functions icon for a physical item, choose Properties, and then choose Specific.

  2. On the Specific tab of the Physical Item Properties page, click the Assign Locator button.

  3. On the Assign Locator page, click a facility in the Facility drop-down list.

  4. Click an area of the facility in the Area drop-down list.

  5. Type a storage locator in the Locator field.

    Optionally, you can specify an ID for the off-site storage facility in the Offsite Storage ID field.

  6. Click the Assign button.

 

Note:
The physical item you are assigning to a storage locator must have a locator type assigned or it will not display the Assign Locator button.

 
 

Tip:
You can assign a Locator to multiple physical items in the Browse View of a workspace by selecting the check box for each item, and then clicking Assign Locator in the More drop-down list.

You can also assign a storage locator to a physical item by clicking the Search Locator button, and then clicking the Select link for an storage locator on the Select Locator page.
