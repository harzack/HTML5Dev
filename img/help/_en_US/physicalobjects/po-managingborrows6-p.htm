To pass multiple borrowed physical items:

  1. Choose Physical Objects Workspace on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Physical Objects Workspace page, click the Circulation link.

  3. On the Circulation page, click the Borrowed Physical Items link.

  4. On the Borrowed Physical Items page, click Pass in the Action drop-down list.

  5. Click the Choose User icon in the Pass To section, and then choose a user.

  6. Click the Choose User icon in the Obtained By section, and then choose a user. Specify this field if the physical item or physical item copy is borrowed on behalf of the user identified in the Borrowed By section.

  7. Click a date in the drop-down lists in the following sections:

    • New Borrow Date, which lets you assign a new borrow date to the borrowed physical items

    • New Return Date, which lets you assign a new return date to the borrowed physical items

    Optionally, select the Pre-Approved Security Overrides check box to automatically approve any security overrides.

  8. Specify any of the remaining settings, and then click the Search button.

  9. In the list of search results, select the check box for each borrowed physical item you want to pass.

  10. Click the Pass button.
