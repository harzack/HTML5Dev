To assign multiple objects to a transfer:

  1. Choose Physical Objects Workspace on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Physical Objects Workspace page, click the Storage Management link.

  3. On the Storage Management page, click the Functions icon for the Transfers link, and then choose Assign Contents.

  4. On the Assign to Transfer page, type a transfer ID in the Transfer ID field.

  5. Type unique IDs in the Unique ID List field.

  6. Click the Assign button.

 

Tips:

  • You can also specify a transfer ID by clicking the Search Transfers button on the Transfers page, specifying search criteria on the Select Transfer page, and then clicking the Search Transfers button.

  • You can also assign multiple objects by scanning a list of bar codes in the Unique ID List field or by clicking the Browse button, and then browsing to a list of unique IDs; or in a Collection or set of Search results, or from the Browse View of a workspace by selecting the check box for each item, and then clicking the Assign to Transfer button (or by clicking it in the More Actions drop-down list).
