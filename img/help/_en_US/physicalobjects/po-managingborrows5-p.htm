To pass a borrowed physical item:

  1. Click the Functions icon for a borrowed physical item, choose Properties, and then choose Circulation.

  2. On the Circulation tab of the Physical Item Properties page, click the Functions icon in the Actions column, and then choose Pass.

  3. On the Pass Information page, type a location name in the Current Location field.

  4. Click the Choose User icon in the Pass To section, and then choose a user. Optionally, type a comment in the Comment field to specify any comments about passing the borrowed physical item.

  5. Click the Choose User icon in the Obtained By section, and then choose a user. Specify this field if the physical item or physical item copy is borrowed on behalf of the user identified in the Borrowed By section.

  6. Click a date in the drop-down lists in the following sections:

    • New Borrow Date, which lets you assign a new borrow date to the borrowed physical items

    • New Return Date, which lets you assign a new return date to the borrowed physical items

  7. Click the Update button.
