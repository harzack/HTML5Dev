To export Table Maintenance or Locator codes:

  1. Choose Physical Objects on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Physical Objects page, click the Physical Objects Administration link.

  3. On the Physical Objects Administration page, click the Import/Export link.

  4. In the Export section, click the Export Table Maintenance Codes or Export Locators link.

  5. In the Export File Name field, type the name for the file to where the codes will be exported, and then click the Export button.
