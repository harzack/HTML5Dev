To remove generated labels in batches:

  1. Choose Physical Objects on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Physical Objects Workspace page, click the Batch Labels link.

  3. On the Batch Labels page, click one of the following radio buttons:

    • Physical Items, which lets you search labels for physical items

    • Locators, which lets you search labels for locators

  4. Specify any remaining search criteria, and then click the Search button.

  5. Select the check box in the first column for each label you want to remove.

  6. Click the Remove button.
