Physical Objects allows you use transfers to track objects when they are moved to a new location. You can assign a single object or multiple objects to a transfer. You can also assign objects in a Collection to a transfer. When you are assigning objects in a Collection, you can narrow the search for transfers by specifying a transfer ID, or a start and end date for objects in a transfer.

Once objects are assigned to a transfer, you can remove them from the transfer.
