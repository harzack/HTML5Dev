When performing advanced searches in Content Server, you can narrow your search to physical item types or physical item attributes. You can search for physical items assigned to a specific physical item type or that contain a specified home location and/or current location code, or you can use Boolean operators to specify a range of physical item types or physical item attributes. For more information about advanced searches, see Searching Content Server.

Upon receiving the search results, the options to borrow and to return physical items are also available (physical items may also be borrowed or returned from a Content Server Collection).
