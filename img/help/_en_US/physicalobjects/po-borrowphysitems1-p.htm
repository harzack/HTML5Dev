To borrow a physical item or physical item copy:

  1. Click the Functions icon for a physical item or physical item copy, choose Properties, and then choose Circulation.

  2. On the Circulation tab of the Physical Item Properties page, click the Functions icon in the Actions column, and then choose Circulate.

    On the Circulation Info page, click a location code in the Current Location field to specify the location where the borrowed physical item is to be stored.

  3. Click the Choose User icon in the Borrowed By section, and then choose a user.

  4. Click the Choose User icon in the Obtained By section, and then choose a user. Specify this field if the physical item or physical item copy is borrowed on behalf of the user identified in the Borrowed By section.

    Optionally, type a reason for the borrow in the Comment field.

  5. Click the date the physical item will be borrowed in the drop-down lists in the Borrowed Date section.

  6. Click the date the physical item will be returned in the drop-down lists in the Return Date section.

  7. In the Acknowledge drop-down list, click an option to specify how borrowers acknowledge receipt of the physical object when it is passed to them or borrowed by them.

  8. Click the Update button.

 

Tip:
If the item is a box or a container, you can view any borrowed children by clicking the Show Borrowed Children button.
