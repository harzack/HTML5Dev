To assign an object to a transfer:

  1. Click the Functions icon for an object, choose Properties, and then choose Specific.

  2. On the Specific tab of the Physical Item Properties page, click the Assign to Transfer button.

  3. On the Assign to Transfer page, type an ID in the Transfer ID field.

  4. Click the Assign button.

 

Note:
The object you are transferring must have a locator type assigned or it will not display the Assign button.

 
 

Tip:
You can also specify a transfer ID by clicking the Search Transfers button on the Transfers page, specifying search criteria on the Select Transfer page, and then clicking the Search Transfers button.
