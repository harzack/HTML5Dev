To receive a transfer:

  1. Choose Physical Objects Workspace on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Physical Objects Workspace page, click the Storage Management link.

  3. On the Storage Management page, click the Transfers link.

  4. On the Transfers page, specify search criteria in the Search Transfers section, and then click the Search button.

  5. In the list of search results, click the Functions icon for a transfer, and then choose Receive.

  6. On the Receive Transfer page, click a date in the drop-down lists in the Receive Date section. Optionally, type a unique ID in the Unique ID List field to specify the boxes you want to receive.

  7. Select the check box next to each box you want to receive.

  8. Click the Receive button.

 

Note:
The transfer is not received until all the boxes in the transfer are received.
 
 

Tip:
You can also specify the boxes you want to receive by scanning the unique IDs of the boxes using a bar-code scanner.
