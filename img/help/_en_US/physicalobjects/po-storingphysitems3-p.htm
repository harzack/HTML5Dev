To remove a locator assigned to a physical item:

  1. Click the Functions icon for a physical item assigned to a locator, choose Properties, and then choose Specific.

  2. On the Specific tab of the Physical Item Properties page, click the Remove Locator button.

 

Note:
You can remove a locator assigned to multiple physical items in a Collection or Search result by selecting the check box beside each item for which you want the Locator removed, and then clicking Remove Locator from the More Actions menu. For more information about Collections, see Using Collections.

You can also remove a locator assigned to multiple physical items in the Browse View of a workspace by clicking Remove Locator on the More drop-down list.
