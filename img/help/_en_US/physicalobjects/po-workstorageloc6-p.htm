To verify the contents of a locator:

  1. Choose Physical Objects on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Physical Objects page, click the Storage Management link.

  3. On the Storage Management page, click the Functions icon for the Locators link, and then choose Verify Locator Contents.

  4. On the Verify Locator page, click the Browse button, navigate to the file that contains the locator data, and then click the Open button. Optionally, in the Create a log file at field, type a directory path where you want to save a log file of the locator data. Optionally, in the results table, ensure that the check boxes of any items that you want to assign to their locators have been selected, and then click the Update All button.

  5. Click the Verify button.

     

    Note:
    Each row within the document must include the following comma-delimited data: Facility, Area, Locator, and Unique ID.
