To generate labels from the Locators link:

  1. Choose Physical Objects Workspace on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Physical Objects Workspace page, click the Storage Management link.

  3. On the Storage Management page, click the Functions icon for the Locators link, and then choose Labels.

  4. On the Locator Labels page, click a label type in the Label Type drop-down list.

  5. Specify any of the remaining settings.

  6. Click the Generate button.
