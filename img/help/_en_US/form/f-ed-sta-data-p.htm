To… Do the following…
Convert a Form into a Stationery Pad Click the Form's Functions icon, choose Properties, and then choose Specific. On the Specific tab, select the Stationery Pad check box, and then click Submit.
Convert a Stationery Pad into a Form Click the Form's Functions icon, choose Properties, and then choose Specific. On the Specific tab, clear the Stationery Pad check box, and then click Submit.
Edit Stationery Pad Data Click the Stationery Pad's Functions icon, choose Edit Stationery Data. On the Edit page, modify the field data, and then click Apply.

For information related to this procedure, see Defining a Stationery Pad.
