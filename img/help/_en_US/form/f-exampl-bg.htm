The Forms examples in this section illustrate how to use Forms, with an emphasis on understanding submission and revision mechanisms. Each example features an overview that presents a common business situation and then demonstrates a way to implement required Forms.

The Forms examples require you to create and work with Form Templates, Forms, and other Content Server items, such as Workflows. The examples provide general instructions, but you may have to refer to other user help topics for specific instructions about how to complete some tasks.
