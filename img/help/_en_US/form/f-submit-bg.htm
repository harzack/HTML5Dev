You process a completed Form that uses a submission mechanism by submitting it. Depending on the mechanisms configured for the Form, the Submit function processes or stores the Form data.

Submitting a Form requires the Use "..." Submission Mechanism permission for the Form. For more information about permissions, see Understanding Form Template and Form Permissions.

Depending on a Form's mechanisms and on the Form Template on which the Form is based, you may have one or more of the following options for submitting the Form:

  • If the Form has no revision mechanism, the button on the Form (such as Save or Apply) that sends the data to the HTTP server.

  • If the Form has both a revision and a submission mechanism, the Submit function.

  • An optional Submit link on the Content Server page that lists the Form.

  • An optional button on the Form that activates the submission mechanism (in addition to the one that activates the revision mechanism).
