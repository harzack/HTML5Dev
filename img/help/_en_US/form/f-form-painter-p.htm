To add a field to a Form Template:

  1. Click the Functions icon of the Form Template, and then choose Edit.

  2. On the Form Template page, in the Add Attribute list, click the attribute type of the Form field you want to add.

  3. On the Add Attribute page, specify the field's properties, and then click OK.

  4. On the Form Template page, click Submit.

For information related to this procedure, see Defining a Form Template.
