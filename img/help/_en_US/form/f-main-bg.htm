Forms are electronic documents you can use to collect and store data online. You can use Forms to make your organization's forms, such as survey information and questionnaires available in Content Server. Before you can add a Form, you or another user must define a Form Template.

You can also use Forms in Workflow Maps. For more information about using Forms in Workflow Maps see Understanding Form Task Steps and Using Forms in Workflow Maps. For more information about participating in Workflows, see Understanding Workflows.
