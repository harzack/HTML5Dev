To edit a field in a Form Template:

  1. Click the Functions icon of the Form Template in which you want to edit a field, and then choose Edit.

  2. On the Form Template page, click the name link of the Form field you want to edit.

  3. On the Edit Attribute page, specify the field's properties, and then click OK.

  4. On the Form Template page, click Submit.

For information related to this procedure, see Defining a Form Template.
