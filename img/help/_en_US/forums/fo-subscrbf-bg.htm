When you subscribe to a forum, you sign up to be notified by email of any changes that are made, such as when new posts are added or existing posts are changed, or when questions are asked or answered in the forum. For more information about questions, see Using Questions and Answers.

 

Note:
To be notified of changes to forum content, you must modify your notification interests on your My Notification Reports page. For more information about notifications, see Using Notifications.

Forum subscriptions are managed by the eLink module, and may not be available if your administrator has disabled eLink. For information about eLink, see Understanding eLink.

 

You can view a list of a forum's subscribers, contributors, and moderators by listing its participants.
