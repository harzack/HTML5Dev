Discussions provide a way to share thoughts and information publicly with other users. Discussions are similar to forums, although not as fully featured. When you convert a discussion to a forum, you replace the discussion with a forum that contains all the topics and replies, including attachments, which the discussion contained. For more information about discussions, see Communicating with Discussions.

 

Note:
Because discussions and forums are governed by different permission types, users' permissions will change when a discussion is converted to a forum, and some users might be granted more access rights than desired. For example, users with the write permission on a discussion will receive the member permissions on the forum. You may want to edit these permissions, as users will be able to modify previous posts and change forum settings, which would not be the case in a discussion. For more information about permissions, see Managing Permissions.

 

You can also convert a forum entry to an FAQ. For more details, see Transferring Content to FAQs.
