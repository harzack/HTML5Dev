Contributing to a forum consists of posting a forum topic (the initial post in a thread), posting replies (subsequent posts in a thread), editing posts, and rating posts. Rating posts allow you to provide feedback on the post using a five-point scale.

When you post to a forum, you can add user references, desktop attachments, and Content Server attachments. User references are users who can provide additional information about the post. Desktop attachments are files that you upload from your computer to attach to the post. Content Server attachments are Content Server items that you associate with a post.

When you post to a moderated forum, your post appears only after the moderator has approved it. Your post is marked as pending until then. If the moderator rejects the post, you will receive a rejection message, which may include a note explaining why the post was rejected.

You can also view a list of a forum's contributors. For more information about listing contributors, see Subscribing to Forums.
