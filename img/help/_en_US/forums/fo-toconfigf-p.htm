To change the settings of a forum:

  1. Click the forum's Functions icon, and then click Change Settings.

  2. On the Forum Settings page, do any of the following:

    • Edit the title or description of the forum.

    • Edit the forum's terms of use.

    • Edit the forum's post warning.

    • Edit and format the header text in the Header box.

    • Edit any of the remaining options.

  3. Click Submit.

 

Tip:
To change a forum's settings from its main page, click the Change Settings link.
