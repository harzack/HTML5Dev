To edit a forum post:

  1. On the forum's main page, click the post's Functions icon, and then click Edit Entry.

  2. On the Edit Forum Entry page, do any of the following:

    • Edit the title and content of the post in the Title and Topic boxes.

    • Click an Icon radio button to change the post's icon from the default.

    • Select an item type in the Add Item list to add an attachment to the entry. Complete the required information and then click Add.

  3. Click Submit.
