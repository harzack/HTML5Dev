Forums are areas where you can engage in discussions with anyone who has the permissions to participate. Some forums are moderated, which means that all posts must be read and approved by a moderator before they appear in the forum. Forums are similar in some respects to discussions, but forums are more fully featured. For information about discussions, see Communicating with Discussions.

In addition to reading and writing forum posts, you can rate posts, view a list of the forums to which you have posted, and subscribe to forums. You can also create and moderate your own forums in Content Server, provided you have the proper permissions.
