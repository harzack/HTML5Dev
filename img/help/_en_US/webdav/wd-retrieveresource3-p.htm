To retrieve a Content Server item using an alternative WebDAV client:

  1. In Content Server, click the Functions icon for an item, select Properties, and then select WebDAV to obtain the item's WebDAV URL on the WebDAV tab.

  2. Do one of the following:

    • Click WebDAV URL on the WebDAV tab of the item's Properties page.

    • Type the item's WebDAV URL in your Internet browser window.

  3. Double-click the item. If the appropriate WebDAV client is available to WebDAV, the item will open in its native application.
