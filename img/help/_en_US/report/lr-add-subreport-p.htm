To add a Sub-report:

  1. Click a LiveReport's Functions icon, and then choose Edit.

  2. On the Edit page, in the Click-thru Sub-Report field, click Browse.

  3. Navigate to a Sub-Report, and then click its Select link.

  4. In the Sub-Report Param <number> fields, type each parameter that you specified in the SQL statement of the Subreport.

  5. Click Save and Execute.
