To configure subscription settings:

  1. Choose Settings on the Tools menu.

  2. On the My General Settings page, click the E-mail Reports tab.

  3. On the My E-mail Reports Settings page, do any of the following:

    • Click the frequency at which you want to receive question activity reports in the Frequency of E-mail Reports list.

    • Click the format you want for the question activity report in the E-mail Message Format list.

  4. Click Update.
