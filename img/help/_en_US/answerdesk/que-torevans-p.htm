To review an answer:

  1. On the question's main page, click the answer's Add Review link.

  2. In the answer's My Review area, click a rating in the Rating list.

  3. Type a comment in the Comment box.

  4. Type an explanation in the Explanation box.

  5. Click Submit.

 

Tip:
To edit your review, click an answer's Edit My Review link, change the rating, comment, or explanation, and then click Update.
