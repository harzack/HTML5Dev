To ask a question:

  1. In the Navigation Menu, click one of the following links:

    • My Questions of either the community directory or your Personal Frontpage to ask a system-wide question.

    • Community Questions of a community, if available, to ask a community-specific question.

  2. On the Questions & Answers or Community Questions page, click the Ask Question tab.

  3. Click the Ask a Question button .

  4. Type a title for the question in the Title box.

  5. Type the full question in the Question box.

  6. Optional Select the Mark Urgent check box to make the question urgent.

  7. Select or clear the check boxes that correspond to the target audience for the question in the Audience area.

  8. To attach a file from your computer to the question, click Browse in the Desktop Attachment area, navigate to the item, and then click Open.

  9. To add an attachment from Content Server to the question, click the Browse Content Server button, navigate to an item that you want to attach to the question, and then click its Select link.

  10. To preview the question before adding it, click Next and then click Finish.

 

Tip:
You can also ask a question by clicking the Ask Question link in the Questions & Answers content module, if available.
