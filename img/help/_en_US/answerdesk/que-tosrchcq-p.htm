To search for a community-specific question:

  1. In a community, click the Community Questions link in the Navigation Menu sidebar.

  2. Click one of the following tabs:

    • My Questions, to narrow the search to your questions.

    • Questions & Answers, to expand the search to all community members' questions.

  3. Type your keywords in the Search for Questions & Answers box.

  4. Click one of the following in the list:

    • all questions

    • answered questions

    • unanswered questions

  5. Click Search.
