When you ask a question, you automatically subscribe to it, which means that you will receive a question activity report by email whenever there is any question activity. Question activity emails are sent when users reply to a question, or when you clarify it, accept or reject its answers, promote its comments to answers, or close it. For more information about question activity, see Working with Questions.

To subscribe to other users' questions, you must first subscribe to a directory in the community directory, to a forum or to a community. You are then subscribed to all questions asked against the directory, forum, or community, and receive question activity reports for activity from those questions. For more information about subscribing to directories, see Using the Community Directory. For more information about subscribing to community topics, see Browsing Community Pages and Adding Content.

You can stop subscribing to another user's question from any of its question activity reports. You can configure your subscription settings so that you are notified immediately, daily, or weekly about other users' questions. You can also configure your subscription settings to format the email as plain text, HTML, or plain text with an HTML enclosure of the report.

The report frequency and message format settings for question activity reports also apply to community activity reports and rated items reports. For more information about community activity reports, see Using the Community Directory. For more information about rated items reports, see Using Rated Items Reports.
