You can view, search for, and reply to both system-wide and community-specific questions. Your replies can take the form of an answer or a comment.

For more information about system-wide and community-specific questions, see Asking Questions.

If you have set up email notifications, you can reply to a posted question directly from your email client. Your response, whether it is an answer or a clarification, will be posted as a comment, with any included attachments, to the corresponding question in the community.

 

Note:
Content Server Notifications must be enabled by your community administrator.

 

When you view a list of questions, you can filter (narrow the scope of) the list to do any of the following:

  • Show only unanswered, answered, urgent, closed, or show all open questions.

  • Show the last 30 questions, current week, previous week, or next week.

  • Show all questions targeted to a particular audience.

     

    Note:
    The audience filter is only available for community-wide questions.

     

You can also combine these filters to reduce the list further. For example, you can choose to show the last 30 unanswered questions, or all urgent questions targeted to a particular audience.

When you view a question, you can read other users' reviews of its answers, or add your own review. A review consists of a rating from zero to five stars, a comment that also serves as the title of the review, and an explanation.
