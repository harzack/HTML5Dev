Questions are requests for information that are broadcast to other community members. When you ask a question, other members can reply and offer additional information. You can view and reply to other users' questions, subscribe to questions, and perform other tasks with questions and their replies. For example, after you ask a question, other users can add answers and comments. You can then accept or reject those answers, and promote comments to accepted answer status if they provide a useful answer the question.

When you ask a question, you are subscribed to it automatically. This means you will receive email notification for any question activity in a question activity report. You can also subscribe to other users' questions. Once a question has one or more accepted answers, you can close it.
