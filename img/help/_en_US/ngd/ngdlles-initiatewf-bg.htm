When you are ready to start work on a project or business process defined by a Workflow Map, you initiate it. When you initiate a Workflow Map, a working copy or instance of the Workflow Map is created and then executed. The work that occurs during the execution of the business process is stored within a Workflow Map instance. Each time you need to perform the same project or business process, you initiate another instance of the Workflow Map. For more information about initiating Workflow Maps, see Exploring the Workflow Map Designer.
