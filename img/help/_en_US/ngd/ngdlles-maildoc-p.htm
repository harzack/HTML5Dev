To mail an item:

  1. Right-click the item you want to mail, click Send To, and then choose one of the following:

    Mail Recipient as Link

    Inserts the name of the item and a link to the item. Depending on your configuration, when the recipient double-clicks a link, the item Overview or Properties page may open in a browser window. The recipient may also be able to open or download the item. The recipient must have permission to access the item.

    Mail Recipient as Attachment

    Inserts a copy of the original item as a file attachment.

  2. In the email message that appears, specify a recipient and a message, and then click Send.
