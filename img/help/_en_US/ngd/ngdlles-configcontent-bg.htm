You can specify when you want to be prompted to enter metadata about items you add to Content Server. You can be prompted to provide metadata only if an attribute or other information is required, or to provide metadata for each item you add to Content Server. Choose the option that suits your best practices for capturing metadata.

 

Note:
You will not be prompted to provide metadata when you add, move, or copy content within a Content Server instance.
