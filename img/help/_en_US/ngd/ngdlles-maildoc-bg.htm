You can send emails that include copies of items as attachments or links to the items. Depending on your administrative settings, the link may open the Overview or Properties page of document, and the Overview page of emails and Compound Emails. The link may also be able to open or download the item. If you include a link to an item, the recipient must have sufficient permissions to view the item in Content Server.

 

Note:
For some types of items, you can only include links, not copies, as attachments.

 
 

Tip:
For information about emailing links to documents using Outlook, see OpenText Enterprise Connect - User Getting Started Guide (NGDCORE-UGD).
