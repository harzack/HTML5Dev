Blogs (or Web logs) are online journals of chronological messages called entries. Typically, blog entries consist of articles, thoughts, commentaries, recommendations, and so on, compiled by one or more authors.

You can subscribe to a blog, rate and comment on blog entries, add and edit your own entries, and create and configure your own blog in Content Server, provided you have the proper permissions.
