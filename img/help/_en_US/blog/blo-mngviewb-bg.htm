In addition to adding blogs as favorites, which allows you to manage a list of your favorite blogs along with your other favorite items, you can view and manage your list of personal blogs, which consists of all the blogs that you have created.

When viewing a blog, you can filter the blog entries to display only entries that meet certain criteria. For example, you can filter by blog topic, entry, weekly archive (all the entries in a given week), and keywords (a series of words that you specify, which must appear in the filtered entries).

You can also generate RSS output (Really Simple Syndication) for a blog. RSS is an XML-based data format that is normally used to publish frequently updated digital content.

 

Note:
Your administrator must enable RSS output for blogs. Contact your administrator if this option is not available.
