When you subscribe to a blog, you sign up to be notified by email of any changes made to its content, for example, when new entries are added or existing entries are changed. The creator of a blog must enable subscriptions in order to subscribe to it. If you have the delete permission on the blog, you can view a list of the users who are subscribed to it.

 

Note:
Blog subscriptions require that the eLink module is installed and enabled. Contact your administrator to enable eLink. For information about eLink, see Understanding eLink.

To be notified of new blog entries, modify your notification interests. For more information about notifications, see Using Notifications.
