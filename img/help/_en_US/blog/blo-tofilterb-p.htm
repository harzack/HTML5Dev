To filter a blog:

To... Do the following on the Blog's main page...
Filter blog entries by keyword In the Filter this Blog box in the sidebar, type one or more keywords and then click Go.
Filter blog entries by blog topic In the Entry Topics sidebar, click an entry topic.
Filter blog entries by post In the Recent Blog Entries sidebar, click a post name.
Filter blog entries by weekly archive In the Weekly Archives sidebar, click a weekly archive.
