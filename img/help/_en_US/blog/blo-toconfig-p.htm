To change blog settings:

  1. Click the blog's Functions icon, and then click Change Settings.

  2. On the Blog Settings page, rename the blog by typing a new name in the Name box.

  3. Add or change the blog description in the Description box.

  4. Change any advanced options settings.

  5. Click Submit.

 

Tip:
To change a blog's settings from its main page, click the Change Settings link in the Blog Console sidebar.
