To edit a prospector:

  1. Click a prospector's Functions icon, click Properties, and then choose Specific.

  2. On the Specific tab, edit the prospector's search criteria or result list options.

  3. Click the Update button.

 

Note:
For information about the parameters you can edit, see Prospector Parameters.

 
 

Tip:
You can also edit a prospector by clicking the Edit Prospector icon on a prospector's result page.
