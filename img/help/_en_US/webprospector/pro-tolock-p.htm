To lock prospector results:

  1. Click a Prospector's Functions icon, and then choose Open.

  2. On a prospector's result page, click the Lock icon in the Functions list that corresponds to the result you want to lock.

  3. Click the Update button.

 

Note:
You can lock only 50% of a prospector's results. If you want to preserve more results, you can take a Snapshot of the results. For information about creating Snapshots, see To Save a Prospector Snapshot.
