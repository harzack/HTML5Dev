To delete a prospector result:

  1. Click a Prospector's Functions icon, and then choose Open.

  2. On a Prospector's result page, click Delete in the Functions list that corresponds to the result you want to delete.

  3. Click the Update button.

 

Note:
In a result, clicking an item's Functions icon, and then choosing Delete deletes the item from Content Server.

 
 

Tip:
If you want to delete all prospector results (except locked results), on a prospector's result page, click the Set to Delete button, and then click the Update button.
