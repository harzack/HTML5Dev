To send an email message:

  1. In the Workflow Map Designer, double-click a Process step.

  2. On the General tab, specify the following information:

    • To, the email address of the user who will receive the message.

    • Subject, the email message subject.

    • Message, the email message.

  3. Click Add to Workflow Map Definition.

  4. Save the Workflow Map.

For information related to this procedure, see Defining a Process Step.
