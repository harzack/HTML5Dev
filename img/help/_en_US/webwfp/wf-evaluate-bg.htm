When you define an Evaluate step, you must by specifying the following information:

  • A step name.

  • Whether Content Server must recalculate the Workflow Map due dates after the step is processed.

  • The conditional statements you create in the Expression Builder that the step uses to determine how to route the Workflow.

For more information about Evaluate steps, see Understanding Evaluate Steps. For more information about Workflow Map due dates, see Understanding Dates and Priorities. For more information about the Expression Builder, see Working with the Expression Builder.
