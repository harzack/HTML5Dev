To… In the Workflow Map Designer, do the following…
Move a link to a docking point Click a link, select the blue square at the start or end of the link and drag it to the middle of the top, bottom, right or left edge of the step.
Reposition a link Click a link, select any of these squares that appear and drag them until you’re satisfied with the shape of the link.
Reposition a step Click a step and drag it to the new location. As you drag the step, a horizontal and vertical ruler appears to help you align the step with steps in the Workflow Map Designer.

For information related to this procedure, see Defining Links Between Steps.
