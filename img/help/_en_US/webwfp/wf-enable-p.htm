To email enable Workflow Map initiation:

  1. Click a Workflow Map's Functions icon, and then choose E-mail Enable.

  2. On the Enable eLink page, in the E-mail Alias field, type the Workflow Map email address.

  3. Click Enable.

 

Tip:
To disable email interaction for a Workflow Map, click a Workflow Map's Functions icon, and then click E-mail Disable.

 

For information related to this procedure, see Email Enabling Workflow Map Initiation.
