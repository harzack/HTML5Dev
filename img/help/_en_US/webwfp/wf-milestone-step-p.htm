To define a Milestone step:

  1. In the Workflow Map Designer, double-click a Milestone step.

  2. Specify a step name.

  3. Do one of the following:

    • Click the Calculate Target Date radio button.

    • Click the Enter Target Date radio button, and then specify the target month, day, year, and hour.

  4. Click Add to Workflow Map Definition.

  5. Save the Workflow Map.

For information related to this procedure, see Defining a Milestone Step.
