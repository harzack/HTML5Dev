To convert a Standard or Advanced Version:

  1. Click a Document's Functions icon, choose Properties, and then choose Specific.

  2. On the Specific tab of the Document Properties page, click one of the following radio buttons in the Versions section:

    • Standard - linear versioning

    • Advanced - major/minor versioning

  3. Click Update.

     

    Note:
    For information about the Max. Versions field, see To Set a Version Limit.
