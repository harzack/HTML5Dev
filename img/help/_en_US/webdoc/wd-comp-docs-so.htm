A Compound Document is a container designed for treating a group of Documents as a whole, and providing them with a specific order and structure. For example, you can use a Compound Document to manage:

  • individual word processor files that represent the chapters of a book

  • a group of HTML and image files that make up a Web site

  • a proposal consisting of spreadsheets, charts and a cover letter
