To reorganize items in a Compound Document:

  1. Click a Compound Document's Functions icon, and then choose Reorganize.

  2. On the Reorganize Contents page, in the New field of each item whose order you want to change, type a positive integer.

  3. Optional Select the Master Document check box for the item you want to designate the master document.

  4. Optional Select the Sequential Renumbering check box to resequence the other items in the Compound Document.

  5. Click Update.
