To access an Appearance in a Virtual Folder:

  1. Navigate to the Virtual Folder that contains your Appearance.

  2. Click the Virtual Folder's Functions button. Select Configure from the Functions menu.

  3. Click the Appearance's Functions button. Select Open from the Functions menu.

     

    Tip:
    You can also locate your new Appearance by selecting Appearance from the Content Filter sidebar. In the Dynamic View page, locate your new Appearance, then click its name.

     

  4. Optional On the <your_appearance_name> page, edit the fields as required. For information about editing an Appearance, see Adding and Editing Appearances.

  5. Optional You must enable your new Appearance in order for it to display in the browse view of a Virtual Folder:

    1. On the <your_appearance_name> page, click Settings.

    2. On the Edit Appearance Settings page:

      1. In the Type field, select whether this Appearance should be Cascading or Non-Cascading.

        Selecting Cascading will apply your Appearance to all sub-items in this Virtual Folder.

      2. In the Current Status field, to enable this Appearance, select Enabled.

      3. Click Submit.
