To change the sidebar settings from the Settings dialog box:

  1. From the Global Menu Bar, select Tools then Settings.

  2. On the My General Settings page, select the General tab.

  3. On the General tab, from the Sidebar Location list, select either Left or Right to determine the default location of the sidebar.

  4. Select the Show Sidebar in Browse check box to make the sidebar appear by default.

  5. Click Update.

To change the sidebar settings dynamically in a View:

  1. When the sidebar is available, but does not appear, move your cursor over the left side of the page until an orange bar appears.

    Hovering over this bar will display the text Click to show the sidebar. Click the orange bar to display the Content Filter sidebar. If the orange bar does not display at the left side of the page, the default setting for the sidebar may have been changed. Move your cursor over to the right side of the page until the orange bar appears, then click it.

  2. If the sidebar appears on your page and you want it turned off, move your cursor between the Browse View and the sidebar until the orange bar appears.

    The text Click to hide the sidebar will appear if you hover your cursor over the orange bar. Click the orange bar to hide the sidebar.

  3. This method of changing whether the sidebar appears on your page is saved as your default setting.

     

    Tip:
    To view your Content Filter default settings, from the Global Menu Bar, select Tools then Settings.
