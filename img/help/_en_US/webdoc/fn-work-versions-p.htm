To work with Versions:

  1. Click an item's Functions icon, choose Properties and then choose Versions.

  2. On the Versions tab of the item's Properties page, do any of the following:

    1. Optional To open a Version, click the Version's Functions icon, and then choose Open.

    2. Optional To view a Version, click the Version's Functions icon, choose Download, and then save the file to your computer.

    3. Optional To download a Version, click the Version's Functions icon, and then choose Open.

    4. Optional To delete a Version, click the Version's Functions icon, choose Delete, and then click OK.

    5. Optional To lock or unlock a Version, click the Version's Functions icon, and then choose Lock or Unlock.

       

      Note:
      Locked Versions cannot be deleted.
