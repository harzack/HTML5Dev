To modify a Search Form:

  1. On the Global Menu Bar, select Search from the Tools menu.

  2. On the Advanced Search page, in the Use this Search Form drop-down list, click the name of a Search Form in one of the following lists:

    • Personal Search Forms

    • System Search Forms

  3. Click the Save as Search Form button.

  4. On the Save as Search Form page, click the Update Existing Search Form radio button.

  5. In the Current Search Forms drop-down list, click the name of a Search Form to modify.

  6. Click Save.

 

Tip:
You can also modify a Search Form by choosing Search Templates on the Personal menu and then clicking a Form's Functions menu. Click either Edit, or Properties from the Functions menu, and then click Specific. You can modify the Search Form configuration on the Specific tab of the Form's Properties page by clicking the Update button to apply your changes.
