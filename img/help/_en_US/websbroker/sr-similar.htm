Finding Documents that are similar to one in a Workspace or on the Search Results page is the most basic way of searching Content Server. Finding similar Documents provides an efficient way to find Documents based on a certain topic. For example, on the Search Results page, searching for Documents similar to one of the top results may be faster than browsing all of the results for Documents on a certain topic.

A search for similar Documents returns Content Server Documents with content, including titles, that is similar to the original Document. For example, if you search for Documents similar to a Microsoft Word document that describes a certain Project, results may include other Microsoft Word documents that discuss the same Project. Results may also include Documents that mention one of the main elements of the Project.
