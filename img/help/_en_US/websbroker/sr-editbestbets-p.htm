To change or remove a best-bets value:

  1. On the Search Results page, click an item's Functions menu, choose Properties, and then choose General.

  2. On the General tab of the item's Properties page, modify or clear the values in the Best Bets Value field, the Best Bets Expiry lists, or both.

  3. Click Update.

 

Note:
An expiry date value applies only to specified best-bet values.

 
 

Tip:
You can also change or remove a best-bets value directly on an item in a container.
