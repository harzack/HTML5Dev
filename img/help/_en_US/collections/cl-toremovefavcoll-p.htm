To remove a Collection from a Favorite:

  1. On the Global Menu Bar, from the Personal menu, select Favorites.

  2. On the My Favorites page, click the Edit/Organize link.

  3. Select the Remove check box for each Collection you want to remove.

  4. Click Submit.

 

Tip:
When you remove a Collection from Favorites, it is removed from both the My Favorites page and from the My Favorite Collections tab on the My Collections page.
