To view the downloaded spreadsheet:

  1. Navigate to the location where the downloaded Collection is located on your computer, and open the spreadsheet.

     

    Note:
    If you saved the spreadsheet using the default naming convention, the file is named Collection export <your_collection_name> <YYYY>-<MM>-<DD> <HHMM>.csv.

     

  2. You can import the downloaded Collection into most spreadsheet applications for review and analysis.
