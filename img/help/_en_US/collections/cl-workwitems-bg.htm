On the Collections page, each collected item has a Functions menu. Depending on the object type, and your permissions regarding the item, you can perform basic functions for the original items, not the pointers, in the Collection. Examples of basic functions include download, open and delete. This allows you to easily access and manage items from the Collection, without having to browse to the items' location.

If you have collected versions of an item, each version is listed and has a limited Functions menu that allows you to perform some of the basic functions on the specific version. In addition, the version number in the Version column on the Collections page is a hyperlink that either opens the Version Overview page, or opens that specific version of the item, depending on your settings.

 

Note:
The options available to you also depend on the privileges assigned by your administrator. Therefore, some of the menu options described in this section may not be available to you, even if you have permissions to the item.

 

For more information about copying, moving and deleting items, see Working with General Items.
