Collections can be added to the Content Server Workspace, to your personal Workspace, or to a Content Server folder.

When you add a Collection to My Workspace, the Collection is added to your My Collections page. A Collection can be added, renamed, deleted or removed.

 

Note:
The ability to create a Collection depends on the privileges assigned by your administrator. Therefore, this option may not be available to you.

 

You can access your personal My Collections page on the Global Menu bar from the Personal menu by selecting Collections. On the My Collections page, the My Collections tab displays the following columns of information about the Collections:

  • Type

  • Name

  • Location

  • Size

  • Modified

Once you have added a personal Collection, you can begin adding collected items. For more information about collecting items, see Collecting Items.

If your administrator provides the appropriate permission level, then in addition to classifying specific items from within a Collection, you can also classify multiple items. For example, you can apply a Classification to all folders within a Collection. Additionally, when you classify an entire Collection, you can send email notification to inform various individuals that an entire collection has been classified. For more information about applying Classifications, see Using Classifications.
