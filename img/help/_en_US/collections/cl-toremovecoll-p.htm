To delete a Collection:

  1. On the Global Menu bar, from the Personal menu, click Collections.

  2. On the My Collections page, click the Functions icon of the Collection you want to delete. From the Functions menu, select Delete.

  3. On the Delete Collections page, click Delete.

  4. On the Deleting Item From page, click OK.

 

Note:
When you delete a Collection, the items in the Collection are not deleted because Collections only contain pointers to items. For more information on working with items within a Collection, see Collecting Items.
