You can view the history of activity within a Collection from the Collection Items Audit tab on the Collections Properties page. The Collection Items Audit page provides detailed information about each event that takes place within a Collection. This includes what items have been added, removed and copied to the Collection, and by what user. Disk Image creations are also recorded, including which Collection items were included in the Disk Image and who performed the Disk Image creation.
