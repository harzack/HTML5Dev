To remove a Hold on items in a Collection:

  1. In a Collection, select the check box next to each item you want the Hold removed from.

  2. Click Remove Hold in the More Actions drop-down list.

  3. On the Search Holds page, type a Hold name in the Search Hold Name for field, and then click the Search button.

  4. Click the Select link for a Hold.

    Optionally, in the Confirm Removal of Hold window, type a reason for removing the Hold in the text box.

  5. Click the OK button.
