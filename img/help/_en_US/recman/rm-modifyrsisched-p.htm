Table 1-6: To modify an RSI schedule:

To... Click the Functions icon for an RSI, choose View Schedule, and then do the following...
Edit an RSI Schedule
 Click the Edit link in the Action section, modify any fields clicking the Continue button to move to the next page, and then click the Update button.
 

Note:
When you edit an RSI Schedule, you can provide a reason why you made the change in the Reason for Update field. The information entered into this field is tracked in the audit trail.

 
Delete an RSI schedule Click the Delete link in the Action section.
