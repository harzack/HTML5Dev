To create a Hold:

  1. Choose Records Management on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Records Management page, click the Hold Maintenance link.

  3. On the Hold Maintenance page, click Hold on the Add Item menu, or to add a Hold to a Hold Group, navigate to the Hold group first, and then click Hold on the Add Item menu.

  4. On the Add Hold page, type a unique name for the Hold in the Hold Name field.

     

    Note:
    Hold names must be unique. If you enter a duplicate Hold name, you will receive a warning message and you must rename it to continue with the creation process.

     

  5. In the Date Applied section, click a month, date, and year in the corresponding drop-down lists.

  6. Click a type of Hold in the Hold Type drop-down list.

    Optionally, type an ID in the Alternate Hold ID field to specify an alternate ID for the Hold.

  7. Specify any of the remaining settings.

  8. Click the Add button.

 

Tip:
To create a Hold for an existing item, click an item's Functions icon, click Properties, and then click Holds. On the Holds properties tab, click Create New Hold in the Apply: Hold drop-down list.
