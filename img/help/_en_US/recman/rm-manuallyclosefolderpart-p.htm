To manually close an RM folder or part:

  1. Choose Records Management on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Records Management page, click the Classifications link.

  3. On the Classifications page, click the Functions icon for a closed RM folder or part, choose Properties, and then choose Specific.

  4. On the Specific tab of the RM folder or part Properties page, select the Closed Flag check box.

  5. Click the Update button.

 

Note:
Closing an RM folder also closes the RM parts within it.
