To manually send a Disposition Review notification:

  1. Click the Functions icon for a Disposition Search, and then choose View Results by Disposition Reviewer.

  2. On the View Results by Disposition Reviewer page, click the Send Notification link for the reviewer to whom you want a notification sent.

  3. Click OK.

 

Tip:
You can click the Send Notification button on the View Results by Disposition Reviewer to send notifications to all Disposition Reviewers.
