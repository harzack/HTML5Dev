To set Disposition Notification email addresses:

  1. In the Records Management workspace, click the Dispositions link.

  2. On the Dispositions page, click the Functions icon for a Disposition Search, and click Properties, and then click Disposition Notification.

  3. On the Disposition Notification tab, select a Content Server user or Group to which you want disposition search results sent, type additional email addresses in the Other Recipients field, and then click the Update button.

 

Note:
E-mail addresses are not verified during this process, be sure to type valid email addresses in the Other Recipients field. If a valid email address is not specified in the profile of a Content Server user or member of a Group, the email will not be delivered.
