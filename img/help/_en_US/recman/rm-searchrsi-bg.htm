You can perform standard and advanced searches for Record Series Identifiers (RSIs) to assign them to classified objects, RM Classifications, and Dispositions. If Records Management is configured for advanced search capabilities, you can construct complex queries based on RSI values, keywords, RSI status codes, or any combination of these.

Once you have assigned RSIs to Content Server objects, you can view a list of the objects for each RSI.
