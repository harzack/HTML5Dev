To create a Justification:

  1. Choose Records Management on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Records Management page, click the Records Management Administration link.

  3. On the Records Management Administration page, click the Justifications link.

  4. On the Justifications page, click Justification on the Add Item menu.

  5. On the Add Justification page, specify any of the settings.

  6. Click the Add button.
