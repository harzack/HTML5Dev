To enable Notifications:

  1. Click Records Management on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Records Management Administration page, click the System Settings link.

  3. On the System Settings page, click the Notifications tab.

  4. In the Notification Enabled area, click the Yes radio button to enable notifications to be sent when records are due for review.

  5. In the E-mail Recipient area, click a radio button to determine whether notifications are sent to the Records Manager of the records or the owner of the records.

  6. Click the Submit button.
