To apply an IRCH schedule to an RSI:

  1. Choose Records Management on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Records Management page, click the RSI link.

  3. On the RSI page, click the Functions icon for an RSI, and then choose Apply IRCH Schedule.

  4. On the Apply IRCH Schedule page, enable the radio button that corresponds to the IRCH schedule you want to apply.

     

    Note:
    If the IRCH schedule has already been applied to the RSI, a check mark displays next to the schedule.

     

  5. Click the Apply button.
