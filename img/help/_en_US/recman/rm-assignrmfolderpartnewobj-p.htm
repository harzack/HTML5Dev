To assign an RM folder or part to a new object:

  1. In the Enterprise or Personal Workspace, click an item type on the Add Item menu.

  2. Click Browse Classifications in the Classify drop-down list.

  3. On the Browse Classifications page, locate an RM folder or part.

  4. Select the check box for the RM folder or part.

  5. Click the Submit button.

 

Note:
An RM folder must contain an open RM part to have objects assigned to it.
