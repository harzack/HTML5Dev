To delete an RSI:

  1. On the Enterprise menu, click Records Management.

  2. In the Records Management workspace, click the RSI link.

  3. On the RSI page, select the check box next to each RSI that you want to delete, and then click the Delete button.

  4. Click OK in the dialog window that appears.

 

Tip:
You can also delete RSIs by clicking the RSI's Functions icon, and then clicking Delete.
