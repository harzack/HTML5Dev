To assign a Hold to an item:

  1. Click the Functions icon for a managed object, choose Properties, and then choose Holds.

  2. On the Holds tab of the object's Properties page, click Search Holds in the Apply: Hold drop-down list.

  3. On the Search Holds page, do one of the following:

    • To search for a Hold, type a Hold name in the Search Hold Name for field, and then click the Search button.

    • Click the Select link for a Hold that appears in the list.

 

Tip:
To assign a Hold to multiple items at once, select the check box beside each item you want to which you want the Hold assigned, and then click the Apply Hold button, or click Apply Hold in the More Actions drop-down list.

To create a new Hold and assign it to the item, click Create New Hold in the Apply: Hold drop-down list in the Search Holds section of the Search Holds page. This option is only available when creating Holds on the fly from a Collection. Otherwise, you can create a new Hold from the Hold tab.
