To view Holds assigned to a user:

  1. Choose Records Management on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Records Management page, click the Hold Maintenance Functions icon, and then click View Holds Assigned to Users.

  3. On the Search for User Holds page, type the user or group name for which you want to view assigned Holds, and then click the Find button.

  4. In the search results, click the link the Actions column to view the Holds assigned to a user.
