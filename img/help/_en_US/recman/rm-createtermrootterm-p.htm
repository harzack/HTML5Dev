To create a term for a root term:

  1. Choose Records Management on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Records Management page, click the RM Classification Terms link.

  3. On the RM Classification Terms page, click the link for a root term.

  4. On the RM Classification Terms Plan page, click the Add New Term button.

  5. On the Add New Term page, type a term name in the New Term field.

  6. Click a relationship in the Relationship drop-down list to specify the relationship between the term and the root term.

  7. Specify any of the remaining settings.

  8. Click the Create button.
