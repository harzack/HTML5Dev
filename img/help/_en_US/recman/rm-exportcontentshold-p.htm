To export the contents of a Hold:

  1. Choose Records Management on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Records Management page, click the Hold Maintenance link.

  3. On the Hold Maintenance page, click the Functions icon for a Hold, and then choose Export Hold Contents.

     

    Note:
    If there are no Content Server items, the following message appears:

    There are no items with this Hold

     

  4. In the Export Hold Contents window, click the Current Version or All Versions radio button, and then click the Export button.
