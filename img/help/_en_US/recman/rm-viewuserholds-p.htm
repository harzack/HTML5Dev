To view or remove users assigned to a Hold:

  1. Choose Records Management on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Records Management page, click the Hold Maintenance link.

  3. On the Hold Maintenance page, click the Functions icon for the Hold that you want to remove users from or view the users currently assigned, click Maintain User Hold, and then choose View/Remove Users.

  4. On the View/Remove Users tab, select the check box for each user you want to remove from the Hold, and then click the Remove button.
