To configure Disposition settings:

  1. Click Records Management on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Records Management Administration page, click the System Settings link.

  3. On the System Settings page, click the Disposition Settings tab.

  4. In the Disposition Reviewer section of the Disposition Settings page, click a radio button to specify how the Disposition Reviewer is determined for items returned in a Disposition Search.

  5. In the Default Disposition Review Decision section, click a default value in the Review Decision list.

  6. Click the Yes or No radio button for each remaining setting, and then click Submit.
