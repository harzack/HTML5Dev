To view and purge Provenance history:

  1. In the Classifications workspace, click the Functions icon for the Provenance item for which you want to view the history, and then click Provenance History.

  2. To purge the history details for the Provenance, click the Purge All button.

 

Note:
If the purging of audit logs is disabled in Content Server, the option to purge Provenance History is not available.
