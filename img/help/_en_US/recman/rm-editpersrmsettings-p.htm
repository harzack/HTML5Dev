To edit personal Records Management settings:

  1. Choose Settings on the Tools menu.

  2. On the My General Settings page, click the Records Management tab.

  3. To hide Records Management tabs on the Properties page of managed items, select the Hide Holds Tab, Hide Records Details Tab, or Hide xReference Tab check boxes.

  4. To specify a default RM Classification for managed items, click the Add button in the Default RM Classification section, navigate to the Classification you want to add as the default, and then click its Select link.

  5. Click the Update button.

 

Tip:
You can remove the default Classification by clicking the Remove button in the Default RM Classification section.
