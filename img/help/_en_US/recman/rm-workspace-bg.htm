The Records Management module allows you to manage documents and objects in Content Server using codes and schedules. The module adds a Records Management item to the Enterprise menu, which displays the Records Management Workspace and provides the ability to create:

  • RM Classifications

  • Disposition Searches

  • Holds

  • Record Types

  • Reports

  • RM classification terms

  • Record Series Identifiers (RSIs) with disposition schedules

You can also perform Records Management administrative tasks, such as maintaining codes through table maintenance.
