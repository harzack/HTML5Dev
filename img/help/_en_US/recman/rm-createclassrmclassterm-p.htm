To create a classification for an RM classification term:

  1. Choose Records Management on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Records Management page, click the RM Classification Term link.

  3. On the RM Classification Terms page, click the link for a root term.

  4. On the RM Classification Terms Plan page, click a root term's Generate link.

  5. Specify any of the settings.

  6. Click the Create button.
