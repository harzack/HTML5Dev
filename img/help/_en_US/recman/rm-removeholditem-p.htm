To remove a Hold on an item:

  1. Click the Functions icon for a managed object, choose Properties, and then choose Holds.

  2. On the Holds tab of the object's Properties page, click the Remove link for a Hold.

    Optionally, in the Confirm Removal of Hold window, type a reason for removing the Hold in the text box.

  3. Click the OK button.

 

Tip:
To remove a Hold from multiple items at once, select the check box beside each item for which you want the Hold removed, and then click Remove Hold on the More Actions drop-down list.

You can remove Holds from items in a Collection by selecting the Entire Collection check box in the detail view of a Collection.
