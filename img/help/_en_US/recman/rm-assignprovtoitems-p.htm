To Assign a Provenance to Managed Items:

  1. Click the Functions icon for the item to which you want to assign a Provenance, click Properties, and then choose Provenance.

  2. Click Browse Provenance on the Select drop-down list, select the check box beside each Provenance you want to assign to the item, and then click the Submit button.

 

Tip:
You can also assign a Provenance to a new item that you are creating by selecting the Provenance in the Classifications drop-down list.
