To add a relation:

  1. Choose Records Management on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Records Management page, click the RM Classification Terms link.

  3. On the RM Classification Terms page, click the link for a term.

  4. On the RM Classification Terms Plan page, click the Relationship button.

  5. On the Add Relations page, click a relationship in the Relationship drop-down list.

  6. Click the Select link for the term you want to add.

  7. Click the Done button.
