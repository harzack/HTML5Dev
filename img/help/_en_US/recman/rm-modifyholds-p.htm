To...
 In the Records Management Workspace, click the Hold Maintenance link, and then do the following...
Edit a Hold Click the link for a Hold, modify any of the settings, and then click the Update button.
Delete a Hold Click the Functions icon for a Hold, and then choose Delete.
Suspend a Hold Click the Functions icon for a Hold, choose Suspend, click a date in the drop-down list in the Date Suspended section, and then click the Update button.
Reactivate a Hold Click the Functions icon for a Hold, choose Reactivate, and then click the Update button.
