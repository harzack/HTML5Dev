To send on a step:

  1. Choose Assignments on the Personal menu.

  2. Click the name of the Workflow step assigned to you, and then review the task instructions.

  3. Review and modify the work package as the step assignment requires.

  4. On the step page, click one of the following buttons:

    • Send On, which indicates that you have completed the assigned task and directs Workflow processing to the next step.

    • A disposition button if a disposition is required for the step.

 

Tips:

  • On your Workflow step Assignment page, select the Finished check boxes to help you track the work you have completed on the step.

  • You can open a step by choosing Workflows on the Personal menu, and then clicking the Assignments tab on the My Workflows page.

 

For information related to this procedure, see Performing Workflow Assignments.
