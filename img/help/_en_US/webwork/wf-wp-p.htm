To… On the step page, do one of the following…
View, modify, or add an attachment
If attachments are enabled in the Workflow step, do one of the following:
•

Click the Attachments link; add, view or modify the attachments; and then click Apply.

•

Scroll to the Attachments area; add, view or modify the attachments, and then click Apply at the bottom of the page.

View or modify an attribute
If Attributes are enabled in the Workflow step, do one of the following:
•

Click the Attributes link, view or modify the attributes, and then click Apply.

•

Scroll to the Attributes area, view or modify the attributes, and then click Apply at the bottom of the page.

View or add a comment
If Comments are enabled in the Workflow step, do one of the following:
•

Click the Comments link, type your comments, and then click Apply.

•

Scroll to the Comments area, type your comments, and then click Apply at the bottom of the page.

Select performers for roles on initiation
If Roles are enabled in the Workflow step, do one of the following:
•

Click the Roles link, choose a user for each role, and then click Apply.

•

Scroll to the Roles area, choose a user for each role, and then click Apply at the bottom of the page.

View and modify a form
If the optional Forms module is installed and enabled in the Workflow step, do one of the following:
•

Click the Forms link, modify the form attributes, and then click Apply.

•

Scroll to the Forms area, click the Edit link to view and modify the attributes, and then click Apply.

For information related to this procedure, see Working with the Work Package.
