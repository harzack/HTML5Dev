To view Workflow assignments:

  1. Choose Workflows on the Personal menu.

  2. On the My Workflows page, click the Assignments tab.

  3. In the Show section, click the Express Builder icon and create a conditional statement to further limit the display of assignments. For details, see Using the Expression Builder.

  4. Click the assignment name link to view the assignment.

 

Tips:

  • You can also view Workflow assignments by clicking Assignments on the Personal menu.

 

For information related to this procedure, see Monitoring Workflows and Assignments.
