Workflows allows users to participate in automatically executing work processes. A workflow is a predefined business process that usually involves multiple users and tasks. For example, a simple workflow can provide a way for an employee to request and receive permission to take a vacation day and may consist of a few tasks that involve the employee, a manager, and a human resources representative. A complex workflow can contain numerous steps and require the participation of dozens of users. In Content Server, when a Workflow is completed, it can be archived for future reference.
