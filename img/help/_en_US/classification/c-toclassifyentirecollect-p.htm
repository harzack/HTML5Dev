To classify an entire collection of items:

  1. Open a Collection from a browse page.

  2. On the Detail View page, select the check box for Entire Collection.

  3. Choose Apply Classifications from the More Actions list.

  4. On the Classify Entire Collection page, click one of the following options in the Classify list:

    • Browse Classifications

    • My Favorites

  5. In the Browse Classifications window, or the My Classification Favorites window, navigate to each Classification that you want to assign to the entire collection. Select the corresponding check box.

  6. Click the Submit button.

  7. On the Classify Entire Collection page, enter the following: Recipient email address, Message Subject, and 3rd Party Notifications, if applicable.

  8. Click the Submit button.

  9. On the Classify Entire Collection Status page, click the OK button when the status shows Done.

 

Note:
The Apply Classifications menu option is only available if your administrator has given you the appropriate usage privileges.
