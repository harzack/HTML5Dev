To classify an existing item:

  1. Click the Functions icon of the item you want to classify, choose Properties, and then choose Classifications.

  2. On the Item Properties page, click one of the following options in the Classify list:

    • Browse Classifications

    • My Favorites

  3. In the Browse Classifications window, or the My Classification Favorites window, navigate to each Classification that you want to assign to the item. Select the corresponding check box.

  4. Click the Submit button.
