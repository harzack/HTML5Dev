Notification is a system that informs you of changes in Content Server. You can specify which activities or items you want to monitor, and at what times you want to be notified via email of any changes. For example, you can configure Notifications to inform you of Tasks that are late every Monday at 8:00 A.M.

Notifications are delivered to Project and personal Notification report pages. They can also be sent directly to your email address.

 

Note:
Setting notifications for the Pulse module is done on your Pulse page by clicking User Settings in the Actions sidebar. For more information, see To Modify Your User Settings.
