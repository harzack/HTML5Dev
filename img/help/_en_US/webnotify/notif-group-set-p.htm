To define group Notification settings:

  1. Choose Users & Groups on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Users and Groups page, click Group Name in the Find list, type the name of a group in the that starts with box, and then click Find.

  3. Click Edit for the group whose settings you want to modify.

  4. In the Modify Notification area of the Edit Group page, click Settings.

  5. In the Notification settings dialog box, modify any of the available Notification settings, and then click Submit.

  6. Click Done.
