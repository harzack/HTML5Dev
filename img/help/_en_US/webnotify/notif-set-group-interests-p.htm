To define group interests:

  1. From the Global Menu bar, choose Users & Groups on the Enterprise menu.

  2. On the Users and Groups page, click Group Name in the Find list, type the name of a group in the that starts with box, and then click Find.

  3. In the Modify Notification area, click Interests, select the report where you want the Notification sent for each interest item, and then click Submit.

  4. Click Done.
