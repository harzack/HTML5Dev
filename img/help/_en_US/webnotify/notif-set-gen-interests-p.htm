To define general Interests:

  1. Choose Notification on the Personal menu.

  2. On the My Notification Reports page, click the tab of the report you want to modify.

  3. Click the Modify Interests button, .

  4. For each interest item on the Set Notification page, click the report where you want the Notification sent in that item's list.

  5. Optional In the Specific Item Interests area, click the link for an item to modify its specific interests.

  6. Click Submit.
