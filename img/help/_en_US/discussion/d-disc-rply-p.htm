To reply to a topic:

  1. Open a Discussion.

  2. On the Discussion page, click the link of the Topic to which you want to reply.

  3. Click the Post Reply icon .

  4. Type a title for the Reply you are posting in the Subject field.

  5. Type the text of your Reply in the Comments field.

  6. Optionally, if you want to attach an item from Content Server, from the Add Content Server Attachment field, click the BrowseContent Server button. Navigate to the item, and then click its Select link.

  7. Also, optionally, if you want to attach a file from your computer, from the Add Desktop Attachment field, click the Browse button. Navigate to the file, and then click the Open button.

  8. Click the Submit button.
