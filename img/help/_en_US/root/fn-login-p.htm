Your administrator will provide you with the username and password required to sign in to Content Server.

  1. Enter your username in the Username field.

  2. Enter your password in the Password field.

  3. Click Sign in.

If you have difficulties signing in to the system, and your administrator has set up notification, there will be a link you can select to send an email to your administrator. This email link will appear at the bottom of the sign in screen.

If your administrator has configured a login screen message, that message will appear above the username field on the login screen.
