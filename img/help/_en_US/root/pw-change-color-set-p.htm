To change your color settings:

  1. On the Global Menu Bar, from the Tools menu, select Settings.

  2. On the My General Settings page, click the Colors tab.

  3. On the My Color Settings page, select a color for each of the following sections:

    • Row 1

    • Row 2

    1. Next to each row, click the blank box to the right of the text field to bring up that row's color options.

    2. Click the color you want assigned to that row.

       

      Note:
      The text field will update with the proper value for the color you selected. The previously blank box to the right of the text field will show the color you selected. The table to the right will show the results of your selection.

       

  4. Click Update.
