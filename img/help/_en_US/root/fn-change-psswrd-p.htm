To change your password settings:

  1. On the Global Menu Bar, from the Tools menu, select Settings .

  2. On the My General Settings page, click the Password tab.

  3. On the Manage Password page, type your current password in the Current Password field.

  4. Type a new password in the New Password field, and then retype it in the Verify Password field.

  5. Click Update.

For information related to this procedure, see Changing Your Personal Settings.
