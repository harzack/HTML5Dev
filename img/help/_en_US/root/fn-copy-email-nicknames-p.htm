To e-mail or copy a nickname URL:

  1. Click any item's Functions icon, choose Properties, and then choose General.

  2. On the General tab of the item's Properties page, in the Short Links field, click one of the following from the list:

    • E-mail the "Properties" short link

    • E-mail the "Open" short link

    • Copy the "Properties" short link

    • Copy the "Open" short link

  3. Click OK.

  4. If you selected one of the email options above, an email message containing the item's name and Short Link will be generated. Your email signature is not included in the email message. In the email message window, type a recipient in the To field. Add any other information you wish in the body of the email before sending.

    If you selected one of the copy options above, a dialog box appears indicating that the short link has been copied to the clipboard. Click OK in that dialog box.
