To rename an item:

  • There are two ways you can rename an item:

    1. Click the item's Functions icon, and then choose Rename.

      1. On the Rename page for the item, in the Name field, type a name for the item.

      2. Click Update.

    2. Click the item's Functions icon, choose Properties and then choose General.

      1. In the Name field, type a new name.

      2. Click Update.
