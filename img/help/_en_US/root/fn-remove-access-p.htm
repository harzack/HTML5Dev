To remove Owner Access, Owner Group Access, or Public Access from an item:

  1. Click an item's Functions icon, and then choose Permissions.

  2. On the Permissions page, on the left side of the page, in the Default Access list, click an Owner, Owner Group, or Public Access link.

  3. In the Edit Permissions section on the right side of the page, click Remove.

  4. Click OK in the confirmation dialog box, and then, on the left side of the page, click Done.
