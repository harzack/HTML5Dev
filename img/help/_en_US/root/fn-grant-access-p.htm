To grant permissions to an item:

  1. Click an item's Functions icon, and then choose Permissions.

  2. On the Permissions page, in the Assigned Access area, click the Grant Access icon, .

  3. In the Grant Access area on the right side of the page, navigate to the user or group whose permissions you want to edit.

     

    Tip:
    For information about how to find a user or group, see Working with Users and Groups.

     

  4. Select that user or group's Grant Access check box.

  5. Click Submit.

  6. On the right side of the page, in the Edit <user/group> Permissions dialog box, in the Access area:

    1. Select the check boxes next to the permissions you want to grant to that user or group.

    2. Click Update.

  7. Optional If you want to edit the permissions of another user or group, proceed to step 2. If you have finished, proceed to the next step.

  8. On the left side of the page, click Done.
