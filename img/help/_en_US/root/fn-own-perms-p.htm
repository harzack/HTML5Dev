To change an item's owner or owner group:

  1. Click an item's Functions icon, and choose Permissions.

  2. On the left side of the page, click the Owner or Owner Group's Change Owner icon, .

  3. On the right side of the page, navigate to the user or group you will be selecting as the new owner or owner group. Click that user or group's Select link.

     

    Tip:
    For information about how to find a user or group, see Working with Users and Groups.

     

  4. On the left side of the page, click Done.

 

Note:
Although you can change the Owner of an item, you cannot remove the Owner or the Owner Group of an item from its access list.
