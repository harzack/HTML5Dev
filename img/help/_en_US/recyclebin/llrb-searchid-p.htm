To generate the search slice you must first locate the Recycle Bin Volume Volume-ID. To find the Volume-ID:

  1. Click the Browse Recycle Bin link in the Recycle Bin section of the Content Server Administration pages.

  2. Click the Functions menu for the Recycle Bin and select Properties. The only selection possible is General.

  3. Be certain that you can see the address bar of your browser window. The value you are looking for is the number after the func=ll&objId= text. Write down this number as you will need it later.

     

    Note:
    To see this number you may have to go to the beginning of the address shown in the address bar of your browser (click the address bar once and press the Home key on your keyboard).
