When a search is done on a Content Server system, all matching documents are found by the search engine, regardless of whether they are in the Recycle Bin or not. To search the Recycle Bin only, a new search slice needs to be generated. To do this, it is necessary to identify the Volume-ID and then generate the new search slice.
