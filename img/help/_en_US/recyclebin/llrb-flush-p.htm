To Permanently Remove an Item from the Recycle Bin:

  • Select the item's Delete function from the Functions menu.

To Permanently Delete Multiple Objects at Once:

  1. Select the check box corresponding to the objects to be deleted.

  2. Click the Delete button.

     

    Tip:
    You may also choose to delete all items within the year, month or day folder by selecting the check box for the container and clicking the Delete button.
