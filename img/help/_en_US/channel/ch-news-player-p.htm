To enable a News Player:

  1. Click a container or workspace's Functions icon, choose Properties, and then choose Presentation.

  2. In the News Player section, click the Select button, and then click the Select link for the Channel whose headlines you want assigned to the News Player.

  3. Click the Update button.

 

Note:
The News Player displays only if there are unexpired News items in any of its assigned Channels.

 
 

Tip:
Click the Remove button in the News Player section to remove Channels you no longer want displayed in the News Player.
