To vote in a Poll:

  1. Click a Poll's Functions icon, and then choose Open. You can also vote in a Poll by clicking on a Poll's name.

    Optionally, click the View Results link at the top of the Poll page to view current results of the Poll.

  2. On the Poll page, cast your vote by selecting check boxes or clicking radio buttons for each of your answers.

    Optionally, type comments in the Comments field.

  3. Click the Submit button.
