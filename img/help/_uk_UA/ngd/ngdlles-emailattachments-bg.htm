If your administrator has enabled this feature, you can specify how email attachments are handled. You can choose to not store separate copies of email attachments, or you can choose to add separate copies of the attachments as individual documents, in addition to the original email and attachments. If appropriate, you can decide, for each email, whether to add separate copies of the attachments, or to save the email and attachments as a single item. The default setting is to not add separate copies of the attachments.
