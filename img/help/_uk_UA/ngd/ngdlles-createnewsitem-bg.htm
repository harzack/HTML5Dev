You can create a News item for a Channel, which lets you publish that item to other users as a news story. For example, you may want to publish a list of new customers as a News item.

You can add News items to any Channel in your Personal Workspace News Page.

A News item consists of a headline and story, and effective and expiration dates to ensure that the News is timely. Also, it can contain an image and attachments. The Attachment icon indicates that a News item includes an attachment.

You access and work with News items from the summary page of the Channel in which they reside.

 

Note:
News items can have multiple attachments. You can add one attachment from Content Server and one attachment from your computer when you first create a News item, but you can add more attachments later by editing the News item.

 

When adding News to a Channel, you can provide a description of the News item, modify its Categories and Attributes, and add the News to a different container.
