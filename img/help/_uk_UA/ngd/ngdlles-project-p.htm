To create a Project:

  1. Right-click the folder or Workspace where you want to create the Project, click Add Item, and then click Project.

  2. In the General Info area of the Add Project page, do the following, and then click Next:

    • Type a name for the Project in the Name box.

    • Click Blank Project in the Create From area.

    • Specify any other general steps.

  3. In the Content area, do the following, and then click Next:

    • Click a status for the Project in the Status list.

    • Click a month, day, and year in the Start Date and Target Date lists.

    • Perform any other optional steps in the Content area.

  4. In the Participants area, do the following, and then click Next:

    • Click the appropriate option in the Participants area.

      Optionally, click Add Participant or Edit Participant to add or edit Project participants.

    • Click Enabled or Disabled in the Public Access area.

    • Click the appropriate option to determine which types of Project participants receive notifications by email in the E-mail Notifications area.

  5. In the Presentation area, do the following, and then click Finish:

    • Click an option in the # of Featured Sub-Items area.

    • Perform any other optional steps in the Presentation area.
