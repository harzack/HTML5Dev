You may want to delete an item if it is no longer used, such as a Discussion that is no longer active.

When deleting an item, note the following:

  • You must have sufficient permissions to delete the item.

  • Deleting a container item, such as a Folder, Project, or Compound Document also deletes all the items within the container.

  • Depending on your configuration, you may be able to restore items that you have deleted. Contact your administrator for more information about restoring deleted items.

 

Tips:

  • You can delete multiple items by selecting and then right-clicking the items that you want to delete, and then choosing Delete. You will receive a message that lists any items that you cannot delete, for example, because you do not have appropriate permissions.

  • If the Content Server Recycle Bin module is available in your environment, you can restore an item that you have deleted by going to the Recycle Bin volume in Enterprise Connect, selecting the item or items that you want to restore, and specifying where you want the restored items to reside. For more information about working with Recycle Bin, see the online help for OpenText Recycle Bin.
