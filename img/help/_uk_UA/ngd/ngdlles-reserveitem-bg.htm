Reserving an item ensures that you are working with the most recent version and prevents other users from modifying it while you are working on it. When you are finished working with the item, you should unreserve it so that other users can modify it.

When you reserve an item, the Reserved icon displays and the item is listed in your Reserved Items folder.

You can reserve Documents, Compound Documents and their equivalents. You cannot reserve an item if it is already reserved by another user.

You must have sufficient permissions to perform this function.
