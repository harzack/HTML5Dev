To define the separator strings between message properties:

  1. In the Enterprise Connect Tree, click a Content Server container, Personal, and then My Settings.

  2. In the Name Generation for E-mails section, in the Separator string box, enter an appropriate value, such as & or -. If you do not specify a value, a single space is used to separate the message properties in the email name.

     

    Note:
    ?, *, /, \, <, >, |, ., :, and " cannot be used as separator values.

     

  3. Click Submit to apply your changes.

     

    Tip:
    Click Reset to revert to the most recently saved values for email naming conventions. Click Use Defaults to revert to the settings provided by the administrator.
