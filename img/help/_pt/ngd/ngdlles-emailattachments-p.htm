To save email attachments:

  1. In the Enterprise Connect Tree, click a Content Server container, Personal, and then My Settings.

  2. In the E-mail Capture Configuration area, choose one of the following options from the When adding email with attachments list to specify how you want to save attachments:

    • Click Do not add separate copies of the attachments if you want to save the email and attachments as a single item. This is the default setting.

    • Click Add separate copies of the attachments if you want to save the email and attachments as a single item, and in addition, save all attachments as separate items.

    • Click Prompt to decide if you want to decide how to save attachments. You can either save the email and attachment as a single record, or save the email and attachment together, and then save an additional copy of the attachment separately.

  3. Click Submit to apply your changes.

     

    Tip:
    Click Reset to revert to the most recently saved values for email naming conventions. Click Use Defaults to revert to the settings provided by the administrator.
