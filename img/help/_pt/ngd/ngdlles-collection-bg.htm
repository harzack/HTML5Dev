A Collection is a single location where you can store pointers to selected items that reside in various locations in Content Server. These pointers enable you to quickly and easily access the original items. You can add items to Collections from your Workspace or from a list of items that have been returned from a search. For example, you could create a Collection that contains pointers to frequently used documents, Task Lists, and URLs and then share the Collection with members of your team. If you have the appropriate permissions and if your administrator has enabled this feature, you can also remove items from Collections.

For more information about working with Collections in Content Server, see Using Collections.
