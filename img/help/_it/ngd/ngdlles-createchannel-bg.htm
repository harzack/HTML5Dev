You can open a Channel to view its summary page, which displays the headlines and stories of all current News items it contains. On the summary page, you can also add a News item to or remove one from the Channel.

Channels do not have permissions of their own; they inherit the permissions of the container to which they are added. This makes it easier for you to direct particular News items to the appropriate audience. For example, if you add a Channel to the Workspace, all users can read the stories it contains. If you add a Channel to a Folder, its News items are available to users who have at least the See Contents permission on the Folder.
