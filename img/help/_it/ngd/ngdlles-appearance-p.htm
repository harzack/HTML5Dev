To create Appearances:

  1. Right-click the location where you want to create an Appearance, click Add Item, and then click Appearance.

  2. On the Add Appearance page, type a name for the Appearance in the Name box.

  3. Specify any other general item settings.

  4. Click Add.

  5. Right-click the new Appearance, and then click Browse from Here.

     

    Note:
    Although the Browse from Here context menu item is available by default, your administrator can remove this item if required. If Browse from Here is not available, contact your administrator.

     

  6. Click the appropriate Edit icon to define the content and the settings of the Appearance.
