To unreserve items:

  1. Right-click the item you want to unreserve, and then click Unreserve.

  2. On the Unreserve page, to add a new version of the item, select the Add New Version check box, and then click Browse to specify the new version you want to add.

  3. Click Submit.

     

    Tip:
    You can unreserve multiple items by selecting and then right-clicking the appropriate items, and then choosing Unreserve.
