To copy a link to the clipboard:

  1. Right-click the appropriate item, click Copy Link to Clipboard, and then click one of the following. The commands that are available will vary depending on the type of item:

    • Click Open to copy a link that enables you to open the item.

    • Click Download to copy a link that enables you to download the item.

    • Click Properties to copy a link that enables you to open the Properties page for the item.

    • Click Overview to copy a link that enables you to open the Overview page for the item.

  2. Paste the link into the target, such as an email, a desktop application such as Word or Excel, or a web browser.
