Depending on your configuration, you may be able to download items to your computer to work with them locally. As a best practice, you should reserve any documents that you download to ensure that other users cannot modify the item while you are using it. For more information about reserving and unreserving items, see Reserving and Unreserving Items. You can also use the Enterprise Connect Download Offline Version command to work with items offline. For more information, see "Downloading Offline Versions " in OpenText Enterprise Connect - User Getting Started Guide (NGDCORE-UGD).
