To search for standard email properties:

  1. On the Content Server Tools menu, click Search.

  2. Under Add to Search Form, click E-mail.

  3. To search on the email subject line, enter text in the Subject box.

  4. To search for senders or recipients, do the following:

    1. Next to the Look For box, select the Recipient check box to search on the recipient name or email address, and select the Sender check box to search on the sender name or email address.

    2. In the Look For box, type the sender or recipient names or email addresses you want to search for. Use a semi-colon (;) to separate multiple entries. For example:

      bsmith@acme.com;lee;kerry jones

  5. To search on dates, click an option in the Received or Sent box.

     

    Note:
    Received and Sent have multiple settings:

    • You must enter a date in the Specific Date, On or Before, and On or After.

    • You must enter a start and end date in the Date Range field.

     

  6. Click Search.

 

Note:
Your administrator can assign different names to the Subject, Received, and Sent boxes.
