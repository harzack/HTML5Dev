Enhanced auditing enables the Administrator to determine the physical objects functions that are tracked in Content Server. You can track the borrow, request, pass, and return of physical items; the addition or deletion of copies, locators, or transfers; the management of codes or storage; the request, return, or date update of physical items; and any location change that users may perform. You can also track objects that are assigned to or removed from a box, and boxes that have had contents added or removed.

For more information about event auditing, see Managing Audit Interests.
