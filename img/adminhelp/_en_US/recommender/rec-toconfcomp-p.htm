To configure a Recommender component:

  1. On the Administration page, click the Configure Recommender Components link in the Recommender Administration section.

  2. On the Configure Recommender Components page, select the Enable check box associated with a component to display it on the Recommendations Page and the Ratings Page by default.

  3. Click the Action button to open the Configure page with the user settings for that component.

  4. On the Configure: <component> page:

    1. Specify the settings that you want your users to see.

    2. Click Update.

  5. On the Configure Recommender Components page, click Update.
