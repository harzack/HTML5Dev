Operations performed on collected items are recorded by Content Server in the Collection Items Audit. By default, these records are kept until the Collections are deleted; however, you can remove older entries from these records in the Purge Collections Items Audit Records area of the administration pages. You can specify the number indicating how many days of information you want to keep. Any information older than the number of days specified is deleted.

For more information about the Collection Items Audit, see Reviewing the Collection Items Audit.
