To configure email settings for questions and answers:

  1. In the Communities Administration area of the Content Server Administration page, click the Configure E-mail Settings for Questions & Answers link.

  2. On the Configure Email Settings for Questions & Answers page, do any of the following:

    • Type a from field suffix in the From Field Suffix box.

    • Select or clear the Enable check box in the Helpful Links area.

    • Select or clear the Enable check box in the Allow E-Mail Replies area.

    • Select or clear the Enable check box in the Send Reports area.

  3. Click Update.
