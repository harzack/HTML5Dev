Content Server Questions and Answers provide Content Server users with a way to request information from other users in a community or across Content Server. Other users can then reply and offer information.

Administering questions and answers includes configuring general settings and email settings.

Users can subscribe to questions, which means that they receive question activity reports in their email accounts detailing what user activity has occurred in the question. You can configure a setting that determines the from address in these emails.
