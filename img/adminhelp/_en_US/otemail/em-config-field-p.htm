To configure email fields:

  1. On the Content Server Administration page, click the Configure E-mail Fields link in the Email Services Administration section.

  2. On the Configure E-mail Fields page, select the Show and Editable options for email fields.

     

    Note:
    Selecting Show for the email field enables the Editable check box. Clearing Show will not show the email field in the Add E-mail dialog box but the email field properties will still be captured.

     

  3. Click Submit.
