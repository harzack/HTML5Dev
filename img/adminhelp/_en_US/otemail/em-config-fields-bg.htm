You can configure user interface options to display specific email fields when users are adding an email to Content Server. The E-mail Subject, From, To, CC, Sent date, and Receive date fields are default fields and appear on the Add E-mail dialog box in Outlook. On Behalf Of and BCC are not default fields and you must configure them if you want them to appear on the Add E-mail dialog box in Outlook.

You can configure whether to hide or show email fields, or to make the email fields editable in the Add E-mail dialog box in Outlook:

  • Show will show the corresponding email field as a read-only field.

  • Editable will make the email field editable. The email field can be marked as editable only if it is marked to Show.
