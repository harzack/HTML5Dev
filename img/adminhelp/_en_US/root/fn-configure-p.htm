To configure a Workspace or container:

  1. Click the Configure icon .

  2. Do any of the following:

    • Type a new name in the item's Name field.

    • Click one of the following view options for each item:

      • List

      • Featured

      • Hidden

      • Delete

  3. Click the Update button.

 

Tip:
You can also configure these settings for individual items on the General tab of the item's properties page.
