Every user who has the Form creation privilege can add new Forms to the system. Once a Form exists, users can grant permission to the Form similar to those for other items.

What distinguishes Forms from most other items is that you must base every Form on a Form Template. A user needs the Form Template creation privilege to add new Form Templates. You control the use of individual templates by granting users permissions for them.
