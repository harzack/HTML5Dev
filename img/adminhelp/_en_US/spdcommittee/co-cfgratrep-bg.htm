Rated items reports are lists, delivered through email, of the ratings that users have given to items created by the report's recipient. These reports help users monitor feedback to the content that they have added to Content Server.

You can configure rated items reports by doing the following:

  • Enabling or disabling rated items reports.

  • Choosing which item types of rated items reports to include.

The item types available when you configure rated items reports are those that have been enabled for the ratings table in the recommender system settings.

For more information, see Using Recommendations and Ratings.
