To configure rated items reports:

  1. In the Communities Administration area of the Content Server Administration page, click the Configure Rated Items Reports link.

  2. On the Configure Rated Items Reports page, do any of the following:

    • In the Send Reports area, select the Enable check box to allow rated items reports to be emailed.

    • In the Item Types area, select the check boxes that correspond to the item types that you want to include in rated items reports.

  3. Click Update.
