To add a community template:

  1. In the Communities Administration area of the Content Server Administration page, click the Open the Community Templates Volume link.

  2. On the Community Templates Volume page, click Community Template on the Add Item menu.

  3. On the Create Community Template page, type the name of the Community Template in the Name box.

  4. Specify any other general item settings.

  5. Click Add.

  6. On the Permissions page, click the Add Role button .

  7. Type a role name in the Role Name box, for example, Leader or Facilitator.

  8. Optional Type additional information in the Role Description and Notes boxes.

  9. Click Yes in the Facilitator list to designate the role as the community facilitator.

  10. In the Permissions area, grant permissions for this role by selecting the appropriate check boxes. For example, if this role is for a community facilitator, select the Edit Permissions check box to grant the role full permissions.

  11. Click Add and then click OK.

     

    Tip:
    You can add additional roles by repeating the steps starting at step 6 in this procedure.

     

  12. Click Done.

  13. Click the <New_Community_Template> link.

  14. On the New Community Template page, point to Facilitator Controls in the Navigation Menu sidebar, and then choose Setup.

  15. On the Configure Community Settings page, configure the template as desired on the following tabs:

    • Homepage

    • Homepage Banner

    • Navigation Menu

    • Configuration

    • Digest

  16. Click Finished.

  17. On the Configure Community Settings page, click the Click here when finished editing link.
