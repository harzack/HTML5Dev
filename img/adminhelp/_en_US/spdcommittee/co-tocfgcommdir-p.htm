To configure the community directory:

  1. Point to Community Directory in the Navigation Menu sidebar, and then click Look & Feel.

  2. On the Homepage Display page, click a module name in the Inactive Content Modules list and then click the Make Active button to activate an inactive content module.

  3. To deactivate an active content module, click a module name in the Left Column or Right Column list and then click the Delete button .

  4. To switch active content modules between the Left Column or Right Column lists, click the module name, and then click the or buttons.

  5. To change the widths of the left and right columns, click one of the following radio buttons in the Layout area:

    • Left Column Large

    • Columns Equal

    • Right Column Large

  6. To add a sidebar below the community directory's Navigation Menu, type a title for the sidebar in the Title box, and then type the sidebar text in the Body box.

     

    Tip:
    To add another sidebar, click the Add Sidebar button , and then repeat this step.

     

  7. Click Update.
