To create a demo template:

  1. In the Communities Administration area of the Content Server Administration page, click the Open the Community Templates Volume link.

  2. On the Community Templates Volume page, click the Create Demo Template link.

  3. On the New Community Demo Template page, point to Facilitator Controls in the Navigation Menu sidebar, and then click Setup.

  4. On the Configure Community Settings page, configure the template as desired on the following tabs:

    • Homepage

    • Homepage Banner

    • Navigation Menu

    • Configuration

    • Digest

     

    Tip:
    You can add additional roles by repeating these procedures starting at step 6 in the procedure To Add a Community Template.

     

  5. Click Finished and then click the Click here when finished editing link.
