To configure email restrictions:

  1. In the Communities Administration area of the Content Server Administration page, click Configure E-mail Restrictions.

  2. On the Configure E-mail Restrictions page, select or clear any of the following Enable check boxes:

    • External Attachments

    • External E-mail Addresses Field

    • Warning

      1. Click the Background Color radio button corresponding to the background color that you want for the warning.

      2. Type the warning text in the Warning Text box.

  3. Click Submit.
