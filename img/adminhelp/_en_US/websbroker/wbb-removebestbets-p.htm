To remove a Best Bets value:

  1. On the administration page, in theBest Bets Administration section, click the Manage Best Bets Items link.

  2. On the Manage Best Bet Items page, type a Best Bets value in the Search your Best Bets items field, and then click the Go button.

  3. Select the check box for each item from which you want to remove the Best Bets value.

  4. Click the Remove Best Bets Entries button.

 

Tip:
You can select the check box at the top of the list to remove all best-bet values.
