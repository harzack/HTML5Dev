You cannot view the Deleted Documents Volume directly. You can purge deleted documents that list all deleted items. You can also search the list of deleted items by creating an advanced Query. For more information, see Purging and Restoring Deleted Documents.

 

Note:
If you do not restrict permissions on the saved Query that you create during this procedure, users will be able to access it.
