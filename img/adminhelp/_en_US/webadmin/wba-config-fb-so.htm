If you are a Knowledge Manager or an administrator, you can access the Facets Volume from the Tools menu on the global menu bar. If you are an administrator, you can also access the Facets Volume from the administration page. If the link to access the Facets Volume is not available to you, then you do not have the necessary privileges. Contact your administrator for access to the Facets Volume.
