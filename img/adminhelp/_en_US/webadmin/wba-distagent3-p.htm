To refresh a distributed agent worker:

  1. On the Content Server administration page, under the System Administration heading, click Distributed Agent Status.

  2. On the Distributed Agent Status page, from the drop-down list on the right side of the page, select one of the time options for refresh.

  3. Click Apply.

     

    Note:
    The selection you make from the drop-down list is not saved. Once you leave the Distributed Agent Status page, the refresh drop-down list reverts to the default Do not refresh status.
