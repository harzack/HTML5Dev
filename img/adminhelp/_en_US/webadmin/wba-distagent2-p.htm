To temporarily start or stop a distributed agent worker:

  1. On the Content Server administration page, under the System Administration heading, click Distributed Agent Status.

  2. On the Distributed Agent Status page, under the Actions column, if the worker has been stopped, click the Resume link. If the worker is running, click the Pause link.

     

    Note:
    The system may resume a stopped worker, or pause a resumed worker, as it requires.
