To list all or a subset of groups:

  1. Log in to Content Server as the user Admin or as a user with the Create/Modify Groups or User Administration rights privilege.

  2. Choose Users & Groups on the Enterprise menu.

  3. In the Find drop-down list, click Group Name.

  4. In the that starts with field, type your search term. Content Server performs a case-insensitive starts with search.

    Typing van, for example, displays groups whose name begins with van or Van.

  5. Click the Find button.

 

Tip:
Click the Find button without typing a search term to display all entries for the specified field. But note that such a search may take a long time and consume significant system resources.
