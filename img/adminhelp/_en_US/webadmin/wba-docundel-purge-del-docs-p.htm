To purge documents in the Deleted Objects Volume:

 

Caution
Once you purge deleted documents, they are permanently removed from Content Server.

 

  1. Click the Purge Deleted Documents link in the Document Undelete Administration section on the Administration page.

  2. On the Purge Deleted Documents page, type an integer in the Days Old field. The default value is 7.

  3. Click the Purge button.

    Content Server removes all documents from the Deleted Objects Volume that have remained there for longer than the number of days specified.
