To set AutoPurge for documents in the Deleted Objects Volume:

 

Caution
Once you purge deleted documents, they are permanently removed from Content Server.

 

  1. Click the Configure Autopurge link in the Document Undelete Administration section on the Administration page.

  2. On the Configure Autopurge page, type an integer in the Days Old field. The default value is 7.

  3. Click the Submit button.
