To configure the Hit Highlight in Summary search:

  1. On the Administration page, in the Search Administration section, click the Configure Search Options link.

  2. On the Configure Search Options page, in the Hit Highlight in Summary section, click the Enable radio button to enable the highlighting of query terms in the document summaries.

  3. Click the Update button.

 

Note:
You must click the Apply button on the Search Bar Edit page to apply your changes.
