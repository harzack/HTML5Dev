On the Solaris 11 operating system, Xvfb is required. Xvfb is included on the Live Media, and more information is provided by Oracle Corporation on the UNIX Implementation Details page of their website.

The OpenText Document Filters installation program does not overwrite the DCSrules.txt file and dcsviewrules.txt file, but sample files with suggested configurations are provided for you as examples in the Content Server_home\config\config-reference folder.
