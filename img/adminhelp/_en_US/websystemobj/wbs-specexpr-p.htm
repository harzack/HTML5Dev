To configure the Enterprise Extractor process:

  1. On the System Object Volume page, click the Enterprise Extractor Data Source Folder link.

  2. Click the Enterprise Extractor Data Flow Manager link.

  3. Click the Functions icon of the Enterprise Extractor process, and then choose Stop.

  4. Click the Enterprise Extractor Process link.

  5. On the Specific tab of the Enterprise Extractor Properties page, edit the parameters of the Enterprise Extractor process.

  6. Click the Update button.

  7. Click the Functions icon of the Enterprise Extractor process, and then choose Start.
