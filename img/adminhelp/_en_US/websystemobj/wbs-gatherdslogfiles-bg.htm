You can gather the search log files and archive them to a temporary log directory. To avoid gathering long files, you can specify the total number of lines you want to collect from the log file. The line number starts at the last line in the file and counts up to the first line of the file. For example, if there are 100 lines in the file, and you specify 11 lines, lines 100 to 89 will be collected.
