Using the settings on the Configure the Enterprise Extractor Settings you can:

  • modify the settings that control the Enterprise Extractor processes when it fails to receive content

  • view items when their content could be obtained

When you modify settings that control the Enterprise Extractor process when objects fail to extract content, you can configure the number of times Content Server will retry to extract an object, the interval between each retry, and the number of times it will retry. Once this expires, Content Server no longer attempts to recover the content for that object. It will send notification that the threshold was exceeded, and you can specify that all Enterprise Extractors be stopped.

Using the Manage Objects That Failed to Have Content Extracted page, you can also manage items that failed to have their content extracted. You can: view the list of items and their locations, see which the items exceeded their limits, retry the extraction process, and remove items.
