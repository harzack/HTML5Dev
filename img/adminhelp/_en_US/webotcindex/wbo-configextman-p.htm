To manage objects that failed:

  1. On the administration page, click the Configure the Enterprise Extractor Settings link.

  2. On the Configure the Enterprise Extractor Settings page, click the Manage Objects That Failed to Have Content Extracted link

  3. On the Manage Objects That Failed to Have Content Extracted page, select the check boxes associated with the items that you want the Enterprise Extractor to reattempt to extract content, and then click the Retry button.

  4. Select the check boxes associated with the items that you want to remove, and then click the Remove button.

  5. On the Manage Objects That Failed to Have Content Extracted page, click the Done button.
