To manage search agents:

  1. On the Administration page, in the Search Administration section, click the Manage Search Agents link.

  2. On the Manage Search Agents page, in the Search Agents section, for each Prospectors or Classifications search agent listed, click the On/Off button to enable or disable their availability.

  3. In the Actions section, for each search agent listed, click Email Search Agent Owners to define the text for the email to send to the owner, with a link to directly access the location of the search agents.
